A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 General Assembly – Fiscal Notes – Impact of Reduction in Health and
3 Community Support Services
4 FOR the purpose of requiring that the fiscal note for a bill that reduces health and
5 community support services funded by the Maryland Department of Health include
6 a certain estimate, assessment, and evaluation of the financial impact as a result of
7 the reduction of services; and generally relating to fiscal notes.
8 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
9 Article – State Government
10 Section 2–1505(a)
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
13 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
14 Article – State Government
15 Section 2–1505(e)
16 Annotated Code of Maryland
17 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
18 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
19 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
20 Article – State Government
21 2–1505.
22 (a) Except as otherwise provided in this section, a committee may not vote on a
23 bill unless:
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.

2 HOUSE BILL 927
1 (1) a fiscal note accompanies the bill; and
2 (2) if the bill affects the funding of a State pension system, an actuarial
3 analysis of the bill is attached to or summarized in the note or the analysis is waived.
4 (e) (1) A fiscal note for a bill shall contain an estimate of the fiscal impact of
5 the bill on the revenues and expenditures of the State government and of local
6 governments:
7 (i) during the year in which the bill is to become effective and the
8 next 4 years after that year; and
9 (ii) if the full fiscal impact of a bill is not expected to occur during
10 those years, during each year until and the first year during which that impact is expected
11 to occur.
12 (2) If a bill, as introduced or amended, imposes a mandate on a local
13 government unit, the fiscal note for the bill shall contain:
14 (i) a statement that clearly identifies the imposition of the mandate;
15 and
16 (ii) an estimate of the fiscal impact of the mandate and, if applicable
17 and if data is available, the effect on local property tax rates.
18 (3) If a bill, as introduced or amended, requires a mandated appropriation,
19 the fiscal note for the bill shall contain:
20 (i) a statement that clearly identifies the imposition of the
21 mandated appropriation; and
22 (ii) an estimate of the fiscal impact of the mandated appropriation.
23 (4) IF A BILL, AS INTRODUCED OR AMENDED, REDUCES HEALTH AND
24 COMMUNITY SUPPORT SERVICES FUNDED BY THE MARYLAND DEPARTMENT OF
25 HEALTH, THE FISCAL NOTE FOR THE BILL SHALL INCLUDE:
26 (I) AN ESTIMATE OF THE NUMBER OF INDIVIDUALS WHO WILL
27 LOSE SERVICES AS A RESULT OF THE REDUCTION, AGGREGATED BY JURISDICTION;
28 (II) AN ASSESSMENT OF THE IMPACT OF THE LOSS OF SERVICES
29 ON THE HEALTH AND WELL–BEING OF INDIVIDUALS AND THEIR FAMILIES; AND
30 (III) AN EVALUATION OF THE FINANCIAL IMPACT OF HEALTH
31 CARE AND SOCIAL SERVICES COSTS TO THE STATE AS A RESULT OF THE REDUCTION
32 IN SERVICES.

3 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
4 October 1, 2025.
