F2 5lr1906
CF HB 611
By: Senator Brooks
Introduced and read first time: January 27, 2025
Assigned to: Education, Energy, and the Environment
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Institutions of Higher Education – Mandatory Disclosures for New and
3 Prospective Students
4 (Informed Enrollment Act)
5 FOR the purpose of requiring institutions of higher education in the State to provide certain
6 information to prospective students and display and present the information in a
7 certain manner; requiring the Maryland Higher Education Commission to make
8 certain data available to institutions of higher education in the State under certain
9 circumstances; and generally relating to the provision of information to new and
10 prospective students at institutions of higher education.
11 BY adding to
12 Article – Education
13 Section 15–140
14 Annotated Code of Maryland
15 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
16 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
17 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
18 Article – Education
19 15–140.
20 (A) SUBJECT TO SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION, ON OR BEFORE JULY 1,
21 2026, EACH INSTITUTION OF HIGHER EDUCATION IN THE STATE THAT ADMITS
22 FIRST–TIME UNDERGRADUATE STUDENTS SHALL PROMINENTLY DISPLAY ON ITS
23 PUBLICLY ACCESSIBLE PROSPECTIVE STUDENT WEBSITE A LINK TO THE U.S.
24 DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION COLLEGE SCORECARD WEBSITE.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *sb0713*
2 SENATE BILL 713
1 (B) EACH INSTITUTION SHALL:
2 (1) INCLUDE WITH THE LINK REQUIRED UNDER SUBSECTION (A) OF
3 THIS SECTION AN EXPLANATION OF THE RESOURCE AND THE INFORMATION IT
4 PROVIDES AS IT RELATES TO:
5 (I) COST OF ATTENDANCE;
6 (II) GRADUATION RATES;
7 (III) SALARY OUTCOMES; AND
8 (IV) DEBT OUTCOMES; AND
9 (2) SHARE THE INFORMATION AND WHERE TO FIND THE
10 INFORMATION ON THE INSTITUTION’S WEBSITES:
11 (I) DURING INCOMING STUDENT ORIENTATION AND
12 RECRUITMENT EVENTS;
13 (II) IN RELATED RECRUITMENT MATERIALS; AND
14 (III) REGULARLY WITH:
15 1. PROSPECTIVE STUDENTS AND THEIR GUARDIANS OR
16 PARENTS;
17 2. NEW INCOMING STUDENTS;
18 3. CURRENT STUDENTS;
19 4. ACADEMIC ADVISORS; AND
20 5. CAMPUS CAREER COUNSELORS.
21 (C) AS APPLICABLE, EACH INSTITUTION SHALL SHARE THE LINK AND
22 EXPLANATIONS REQUIRED UNDER SUBSECTIONS (A) AND (B) OF THIS SECTION ON:
23 (1) THE INSTITUTION’S SOCIAL MEDIA ACCOUNTS AT LEAST ONCE
24 EACH SEMESTER; AND
25 (2) THE WEBSITE FOR THE INSTITUTION’S CAMPUS CAREER CENTER.
SENATE BILL 713 3
1 (D) (1) IF THE U.S. DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION DISCONTINUES THE
2 OPERATION OF THE COLLEGE SCORECARD, THE COMMISSION SHALL MAKE THE
3 DATA REQUIRED UNDER SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION AVAILABLE TO
4 INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER EDUCATION IN THE STATE.
5 (2) IN COMPILING THE DATA UNDER THIS SUBSECTION, THE
6 COMMISSION SHALL:
7 (I) COLLABORATE WITH INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER
8 EDUCATION; AND
9 (II) USE DATA MAINTAINED BY:
10 1. THE COMMISSION;
11 2. THE MARYLAND LONGITUDINAL DATA SYSTEM; AND
12 3. OTHER RELATED SOURCES.
13 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
14 1, 2025.
