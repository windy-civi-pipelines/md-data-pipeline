By: Senator Muse
Requested: October 2, 2024
Introduced and read first time: January 8, 2025
Assigned to: Judicial Proceedings
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 State Highway Administration – Memorials – Maryland Route 210 in Prince
3 George’s County
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the State Highway Administration to erect a memorial sign
5 on a certain portion of Maryland Route 210 in Prince George’s County remembering
6 individuals who have died in motor vehicle accidents on Maryland Route 210; and
7 generally relating to a memorial remembering individuals who have died in motor
8 vehicle accidents on Maryland Route 210.
9 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
10 Article – Transportation
11 Section 8–663
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
14 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
16 Article – Transportation
17 8–663.
18 (A) The Administration shall designate Maryland Route 210 as the Piscataway
19 Highway.
20 (B) (1) THE ADMINISTRATION SHALL ERECT A MEMORIAL SIGN ALONG
21 THE PORTION OF MARYLAND ROUTE 210 THAT IS LOCATED BETWEEN MILE
22 MARKERS 11.359 AND 11.672, RAMPS 1 AND 2 TO KERBY HILL ROAD,
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *sb0193*
2 SENATE BILL 193
1 REMEMBERING INDIVIDUALS WHO HAVE DIED FROM INJURIES SUSTAINED IN
2 MOTOR VEHICLE ACCIDENTS ON MARYLAND ROUTE 210.
3 (2) THE MEMORIAL SIGN SHALL:
4 (I) BE VISIBLE TO TRAVELERS ON MARYLAND ROUTE 210; AND
5 (II) INCLUDE THE FOLLOWING LANGUAGE:
6 “IN MEMORY OF THE LIVES LOST ON MARYLAND ROUTE 210. WE ARE MAKING OUR
7 HIGHWAY SAFER.”.
8 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
9 October 1, 2025.
