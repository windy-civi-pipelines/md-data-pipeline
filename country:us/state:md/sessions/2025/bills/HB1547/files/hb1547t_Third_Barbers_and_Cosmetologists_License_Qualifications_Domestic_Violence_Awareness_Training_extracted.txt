CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Barbers and Cosmetologists – License Qualifications – Domestic Violence
3 Awareness Training
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the State Board of Barbers and the State Board of
5 Cosmetologists to require as a qualification for licensure an applicant to complete
6 certain training in domestic violence awareness; requiring the Maryland
7 Department of Labor to develop and administer certain domestic violence awareness
8 training that includes certain guidance; requiring the State Board of Barbers and
9 the State Board of Cosmetologists to require certain licensees to complete a one–time
10 training requirement to renew a certain license; and generally relating to licensing
11 qualifications for barbers and cosmetologists.
12 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
13 Article – Business Occupations and Professions
14 Section 4–201 and 5–201
15 Annotated Code of Maryland
16 (2018 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
17 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
18 Article – Business Occupations and Professions
19 Section 4–302, 5–304, and 5–305
20 Annotated Code of Maryland
21 (2018 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
