A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Workers’ Compensation – Rehabilitation Practitioners – Licensed Certified
3 Social Worker–Clinical
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing a licensed certified social worker–clinical to register as a
5 rehabilitation practitioner; providing that a licensed certified social worker–clinical
6 is not subject to certain supervision requirements; and generally relating to
7 rehabilitation practitioners.
8 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
9 Article – Labor and Employment
10 Section 9–6A–01(a)
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2016 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
13 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
14 Article – Labor and Employment
15 Section 9–6A–01(d), 9–6A–04, and 9–6A–09(d)
16 Annotated Code of Maryland
17 (2016 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
18 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
19 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
20 Article – Labor and Employment
21 9–6A–01.
22 (a) In this subtitle the following words have the meanings indicated.
23 (d) (1) “Rehabilitation practitioner” means an individual who provides
24 vocational rehabilitation services.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb1404*
2 HOUSE BILL 1404
1 (2) “Rehabilitation practitioner” includes:
2 (i) a nurse certified by the State Board of Nursing as a nurse case
3 manager;
4 (ii) a rehabilitation counselor; [and]
5 (iii) a vocational evaluator; AND
6 (IV) A LICENSED CERTIFIED SOCIAL WORKER–CLINICAL.
7 9–6A–04.
8 (a) This subtitle does not limit the right of any individual to engage in a health
9 occupation that the individual otherwise is authorized to engage in under the Health
10 Occupations Article.
11 (b) The Commission has the authority to adopt and enforce rules and regulations
12 regarding the standards of practice of rehabilitation counselors and vocational evaluators.
13 (c) Violation of the rules and regulations adopted or enforced by the Commission,
14 THE STATE BOARD OF SOCIAL WORK EXAMINERS, or the State Board of Nursing in
15 accordance with this title by a rehabilitation practitioner shall be grounds for denial of a
16 registration to any applicant, or suspension or revocation of a registrant’s registration
17 pursuant to §§ 9–6A–15 and 9–6A–16 of this subtitle.
18 9–6A–09.
19 (d) (1) THIS SUBSECTION DOES NOT APPLY TO A LICENSED CERTIFIED
20 SOCIAL WORKER–CLINICAL.
21 (2) In addition to the requirements of subsections (b) and (c) of this section:
22 [(1)] (I) a rehabilitation counselor who has met the education
23 requirements under subsection (b)(1) or (2) of this section to qualify for registration shall
24 work under the administrative supervision of a certified rehabilitation counselor, certified
25 vocational evaluator, certified disability management specialist, certified case manager, or
26 certified rehabilitation registered nurse; and
27 [(2)] (II) a vocational evaluator who has met the education requirements
28 under subsection (c)(1) or (2) of this section shall work under the administrative supervision
29 of a certified vocational evaluator, certified rehabilitation counselor, certified disability
30 management specialist, certified case manager, or certified rehabilitation registered nurse.
HOUSE BILL 1404 3
1 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
2 October 1, 2025.
