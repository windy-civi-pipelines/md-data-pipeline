CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Solid Waste Disposal Surcharge On–Farm Organics and Wasted Food Reduction
3 and Diversion Fund and Grant Programs – Established
4 FOR the purpose of establishing the On–Farm Organics Diversion and Recycling Grant
5 Program in the Department of Agriculture to award grants to eligible entities to
6 develop and implement on–farm organics recycling and compost use, wasted food
7 prevention, and food rescue; establishing a solid waste disposal surcharge on the
8 final disposal of solid waste in the State; requiring, beginning on a certain date and
9 each quarter thereafter, the owner or operator of a refuse disposal system to submit
10 a certain solid waste disposal surcharge to the Comptroller in a certain manner;
11 requiring the Comptroller to deposit certain surcharges into a certain fund;
12 establishing the Wasted Food Reduction and Diversion Fund as a special, nonlapsing
13 fund and requiring interest earnings of the Fund to be credited to the Fund;
14 establishing the Wasted Food Reduction and Diversion Grant Program and the
15 County Wasted Food Reduction Block Grant Program in the Department of the
16 Environment to award grants and fund certain projects for infrastructure, programs,
17 and education that reduce, rescue, and divert wasted food in the State and certain
18 counties; authorizing the Governor to transfer a certain amount from the Maryland
19 Strategic Energy Investment Fund to certain programs in certain fiscal years; and
20 generally relating to wasted food reduction and diversion in the State.
