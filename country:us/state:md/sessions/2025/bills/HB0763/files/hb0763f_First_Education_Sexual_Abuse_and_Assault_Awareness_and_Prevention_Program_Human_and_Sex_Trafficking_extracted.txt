A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Education – Sexual Abuse and Assault Awareness and Prevention Program –
3 Human and Sex Trafficking
4 FOR the purpose of requiring that a certain program on the awareness and prevention of
5 sexual abuse and assault include, for students in certain grades, material promoting
6 the awareness and prevention of human and sex trafficking; and generally relating
7 to human and sex trafficking awareness and prevention programs in public and
8 nonpublic schools.
9 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
10 Article – Education
11 Section 7–439
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
14 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
16 Article – Education
17 7–439.
18 (a) In this section, “nonpublic school” means a noncollegiate educational
19 institution that:
20 (1) Holds a certificate of approval from the State Board under § 2–206(e) of
21 this article; and
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.

1 (2) Participates in the Nonpublic Schools Textbook and Technology Grants
2 Program.
3 (b) (1) The State Board and each nonpublic school in the State shall develop
4 and implement a program of age–appropriate education on the awareness and prevention
5 of sexual abuse and assault.
6 (2) The program required under paragraph (1) of this subsection shall [be]:
7 (i) [Taught] BE TAUGHT by a teacher who is trained to provide
8 instruction on the awareness and prevention of sexual abuse and assault; [and]
9 (ii) [Incorporated] BE INCORPORATED into the health curriculum
10 of each county board and each nonpublic school; AND
11 (III) INCLUDE, FOR STUDENTS IN GRADES 6 THROUGH 8,
12 MATERIAL PROMOTING THE AWARENESS AND PREVENTION OF HUMAN AND SEX
13 TRAFFICKING.
14 (c) The State Board shall adopt regulations to carry out the provisions of this
15 section.
16 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
17 1, 2025.
