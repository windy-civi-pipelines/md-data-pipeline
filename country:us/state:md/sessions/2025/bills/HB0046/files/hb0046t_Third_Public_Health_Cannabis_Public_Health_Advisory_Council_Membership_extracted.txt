2 HOUSE BILL 46
1 (a) There is a Cannabis Public Health Advisory Council.
2 (b) The Advisory Council consists of the following members:
3 (1) One member from the Senate of Maryland, appointed by the President
4 of the Senate;
5 (2) One member from the House of Delegates, appointed by the Speaker of
6 the House;
7 (3) The Secretary, or the Secretary’s designee;
8 (4) The Deputy Secretary for Behavioral Health, or the Deputy Secretary’s
9 designee;
10 (5) The Secretary of Agriculture, or the Secretary’s designee;
11 (6) The Executive Director of the [Natalie M. LaPrade Medical Cannabis
12 Commission] MARYLAND CANNABIS ADMINISTRATION, or the Executive Director’s
13 designee;
14 (7) The State Superintendent of Schools, or the State Superintendent’s
15 designee;
16 (8) The Executive Director of the Governor’s Office of Crime Prevention
17 and Policy, or the Executive Director’s designee; [and]
18 (9) THE ADMINISTRATOR OF THE MOTOR VEHICLE
19 ADMINISTRATION, OR THE ADMINISTRATOR’S DESIGNEE; AND
20 (10) The following members appointed by the Governor:
21 (i) One representative from a historically black college or
22 university;
23 (ii) One health care provider with experience in cannabis;
24 (iii) One pharmacist licensed in the State;
25 (iv) One health care provider with expertise in substance use
26 disorder treatment and recovery;
27 (v) One individual with expertise in cannabis use disorder;
HOUSE BILL 46 3
1 (vi) One academic researcher with expertise in cannabis law and
2 policy;
3 (vii) One individual with at least 5 years of experience in health or
4 social equity;
5 (viii) One public health professional with cannabis experience; and
6 (ix) One representative of a laboratory that tests cannabis.
7 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
8 October 1, 2025.
Approved:
