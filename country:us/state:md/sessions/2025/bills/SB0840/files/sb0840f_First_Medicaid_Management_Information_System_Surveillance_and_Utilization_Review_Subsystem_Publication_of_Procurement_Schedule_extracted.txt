Section 1:
J1, P2 5lr3460
By: Senator Mautz
Introduced and read first time: January 28, 2025
Assigned to: Finance
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Medicaid Management Information System – Surveillance and Utilization
3 Review Subsystem – Publication of Procurement Schedule
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Maryland Department of Health to publish on its website
5 a schedule for the issuance of a solicitation for the procurement of a Surveillance and
6 Utilization Review Subsystem of the Medicaid Management Information System;
7 requiring the Department to publish on its website notice of certain time frames at
8 least a certain number of days before the issuance of the procurement; and generally
9 relating to the procurement of a Surveillance and Utilization Review Subsystem of
10 the Medicaid Management Information System.
11 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
12 That:
13 (a) On or before October 1, 2025, the Maryland Department of Health shall
14 publish on its website a schedule for the issuance of a solicitation for the procurement of a
15 Surveillance and Utilization Review Subsystem of the Medicaid Management Information
16 System.
17 (b) At least 60 days before the issuance of a solicitation described under
18 subsection (a) of this section, the Maryland Department of Health shall publish on its
19 website written notice of the contracting procedures, requirements, and timetables.
20 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
21 1, 2025.
