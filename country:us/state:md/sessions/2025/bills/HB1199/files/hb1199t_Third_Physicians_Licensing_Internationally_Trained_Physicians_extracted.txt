17 (3) HAD NO DISCIPLINE OR COMPETENCY ISSUES RAISED DURING
18 THE APPLICANT’S POSTGRADUATE TRAINING;
19 (4) (3) HAS BEEN LICENSED OR OTHERWISE AUTHORIZED TO
20 PRACTICE MEDICINE IN A COUNTRY OTHER THAN THE UNITED STATES, ITS
21 TERRITORIES OR POSSESSIONS, PUERTO RICO, OR CANADA, AND HAS PRACTICED
22 MEDICINE FOR AT LEAST 5 OF THE 7 YEARS IMMEDIATELY PRECEDING THE DATE OF
23 APPLICATION;
24 (5) (4) IS IN GOOD STANDING WITH THE MEDICAL LICENSING OR
25 REGULATORY AUTHORITY IN THE COUNTRY OTHER THAN THE UNITED STATES, ITS
26 TERRITORIES OR POSSESSIONS, PUERTO RICO, OR CANADA WITH WHICH THE
27 BOARD, THE FEDERATION OF STATE MEDICAL BOARDS, OR ANOTHER NATIONALLY
28 RECOGNIZED MEDICAL STANDARDS OVERSIGHT ENTITY IS ABLE TO VERIFY THE
29 PHYSICIAN’S DISCIPLINE AND CRIMINAL BACKGROUND HISTORY;
30 (6) (5) HAS NOT BEEN PREVIOUSLY DISCIPLINED AND HAS NO
31 PENDING DISCIPLINARY MATTERS BEFORE ANY LICENSING OR REGULATORY BODY;
32 (7) (6) HAS A VALID CERTIFICATE ISSUED BY THE EDUCATIONAL
33 COMMISSION FOR FOREIGN MEDICAL GRADUATES;

1 (8) (7) HAS ACHIEVED A PASSING SCORE ON STEP 1, STEP 2

3 LICENSING EXAMINATION;

5 (8) IS DOMICILED IN THE STATE;

7 ENGLISH LANGUAGE;

9 (12) (11) MEETS THE REQUIREMENTS UNDER §§ 14–309 AND 14–313
10 OF THIS SUBTITLE; AND
11 (13) (12) HAS SATISFIED ANY OTHER CRITERIA ESTABLISHED BY THE
12 BOARD FOR ISSUANCE OF A LICENSE UNDER THIS SECTION.
13 (C) THE BOARD MAY DETERMINE AN APPLICANT INELIGIBLE FOR
14 LICENSURE IF THE APPLICANT HAD:
15 (1) A PREVIOUS DISCIPLINARY ACTION; OR
16 (2) DISCIPLINE OR COMPETENCY ISSUES DURING THE APPLICANT’S
17 POSTGRADUATE TRAINING.
18 (C) (D) THE BOARD MAY REVOKE A LICENSE ISSUED UNDER THIS
19 SECTION IF:
20 (1) THE LICENSEE HAS PRACTICED OUTSIDE THE SCOPE OF THE
21 LICENSE;
22 (2) THE LICENSEE’S EMPLOYMENT IS TERMINATED;
23 (3) THE LICENSEE HAS BEEN THE SUBJECT OF A DISCIPLINARY
24 ACTION; OR
25 (4) THE LICENSEE IS NO LONGER ELIGIBLE FOR THE LICENSE.
26 (D) (E) THE BOARD SHALL ADOPT REGULATIONS:
27 (1) TO ESTABLISH MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS AND APPLICATION
28 FEES FOR A LICENSE ISSUED UNDER THIS SECTION;
4 HOUSE BILL 1199
1 (2) TO ESTABLISH CONDITIONS APPLICABLE TO A LICENSEE;
2 (3) TO PROVIDE A PATHWAY TO FULL LICENSURE AFTER THE
3 LICENSEE SUCCESSFULLY SATISFIES ANY LICENSE CONDITIONS ESTABLISHED BY
4 THE BOARD UNDER ITEM (2) OF THIS SUBSECTION; AND
5 (4) NECESSARY FOR THE IMPLEMENTATION, ADMINISTRATION, AND
6 ENFORCEMENT OF THIS SECTION.
7 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
8 October 1, 2025.
