1 AN ACT concerning
2 Labor and Employment – Occupational Safety and Health – Revisions
3 (Davis Martinez Public Employee Safety and Health Act)
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Correctional Training Commission to adopt certain
5 regulations for the training, issuance, and use of body–worn cameras; requiring each
6 correctional unit to develop and maintain a certain written policy for the use of
7 body–worn cameras by correctional officers, subject to a certain exception;
8 establishing that it is lawful for a correctional officer to intercept certain
9 communications by use of a body–worn camera under certain circumstances;
10 requiring the Commissioner of Labor and Industry to appoint an Assistant
11 Commissioner for Public Employees’ Safety and Health for certain purposes;
12 establishing the Public Employees’ Safety and Health Unit in the Division of Labor
13 and Industry to administer and enforce certain provisions regarding the oversight of
14 workplace safety and health of employees of certain public bodies; altering the
15 Maryland Occupational Safety and Health Act as it applies to certain public bodies;
