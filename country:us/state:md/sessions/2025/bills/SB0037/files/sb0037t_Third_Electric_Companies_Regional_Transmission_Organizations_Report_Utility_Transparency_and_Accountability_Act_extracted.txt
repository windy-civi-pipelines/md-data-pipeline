CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Electric Companies – Regional Transmission Organizations – Report
3 (Utility Transparency and Accountability Act)
4 FOR the purpose of requiring a certain electric company to submit an annual report to the
5 Public Service Commission regarding each recorded vote cast by the electric company
6 and any State affiliate of the electric company at a meeting of a regional transmission
7 organization; and generally relating to electric companies and regional transmission
8 organizations.
9 BY adding to
10 Article – Public Utilities
11 Section 7–108
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
14 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
16 Article – Public Utilities
17 7–108.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from existing law.
Underlining indicates amendments to bill.
Strike out indicates matter stricken from the bill by amendment or deleted from the law by
amendment. *sb0037*
2 SENATE BILL 37
1 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
2 INDICATED.
3 (2) “AFFILIATE” HAS THE MEANING STATED IN § 7–501 OF THIS
4 TITLE.
5 (3) “MEETING” MEANS ANY COMMITTEE, USER GROUP, TASK FORCE,
6 OR OTHER PART OF THE REGIONAL TRANSMISSION ORGANIZATION IN WHICH VOTES
7 ARE TAKEN.
8 (4) “RECORDED VOTE” MEANS A VOTE THAT IS TABULATED, EITHER
9 INDIVIDUALLY OR AS PART OF A SECTOR, FOR ANY PURPOSE AT A MEETING,
10 REGARDLESS OF:
11 (I) WHETHER THE VOTE REPRESENTS A FINAL POSITION OF
12 ANY PERSON CASTING THE VOTE; OR
13 (II) THE DECISION–MAKING AUTHORITY OF THOSE VOTING.
14 (5) “REGIONAL TRANSMISSION ORGANIZATION” MEANS AN ENTITY
15 THAT QUALIFIES AS A REGIONAL TRANSMISSION ORGANIZATION UNDER 18 C.F.R. §
16 35.34.
17 (B) THIS SECTION DOES NOT APPLY TO A MUNICIPAL ELECTRIC UTILITY.
18 (C) (1) ON OR BEFORE FEBRUARY 1 EACH YEAR, EACH ELECTRIC
19 COMPANY SHALL SUBMIT TO THE COMMISSION A REPORT ON EACH RECORDED VOTE
20 CAST BY THE ELECTRIC COMPANY OR, SUBJECT TO PARAGRAPH (2) OF THIS
21 SUBSECTION, AN A STATE AFFILIATE OF THE ELECTRIC COMPANY AT A MEETING OF
22 A REGIONAL TRANSMISSION ORGANIZATION DURING THE IMMEDIATELY PRECEDING
23 CALENDAR YEAR.
24 (2) THE REPORT SHALL INCLUDE:
25 (I) ALL RECORDED VOTES CAST BY THE ELECTRIC COMPANY,
26 REGARDLESS OF WHETHER THE VOTE IS OTHERWISE DISCLOSED; AND
27 (II) ALL VOTES CAST BY AN A STATE AFFILIATE OF THE
28 ELECTRIC COMPANY IF THE ELECTRIC COMPANY ITSELF DOES NOT VOTE ON THE
29 MATTER.
30 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
31 October 1, 2025.
