A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Charles County – Gaming – Video Lottery Operation License and Video Lottery
3 Terminals
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing the awarding of an additional video lottery operation
5 license and a certain number of additional video lottery terminals for a video lottery
6 facility at a certain location in Charles County; authorizing the Governor to
7 reconstitute the Video Lottery Facility Location Commission for a certain purpose;
8 submitting this Act to a referendum of the qualified voters of the State; and generally
9 relating to video lottery operation licenses and video lottery terminals in Charles
10 County.
11 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
12 Article – State Government
13 Section 9–1A–02(d)
14 Annotated Code of Maryland

17 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That the provisions of Section 1 of
18 this Act authorize the Video Lottery Facility Location Commission to award one video
19 lottery operation license in Charles County with the authorization subject to a referendum
20 of the qualified voters of Maryland as provided in Section 3 of this Act.
21 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That before this Act, which
22 authorizes the additional forms or expansion of commercial gaming, becomes effective, it
23 shall first be submitted to a referendum of the qualified voters of the State at the general
24 election to be held in November 2026, in accordance with Article XIX, § 1(e) of the Maryland
25 Constitution. The State Board of Elections shall do those things necessary and proper to
26 provide for and hold the referendum required by this section. If a majority of the votes cast
27 on the question are “For the referred law”, this Act shall become effective on the 30th day
28 following the official canvass of votes for the referendum, but if a majority of the votes cast
29 on the question are “Against the referred law”, this Act, with no further action required by
30 the General Assembly, shall be null and void.
31 SECTION 4. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That, subject to Section 3 of this
32 Act and for the sole purpose of providing for the referendum required by Section 3 of this
33 Act, this Act shall take effect July 1, 2025.
