Section 1:
M2 5lr3413
CF 5lr3416
By: Senator Mautz
Introduced and read first time: January 28, 2025
Assigned to: Education, Energy, and the Environment
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Natural Resources – Public Clam Fishery Area – Establishment
3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Department of Natural Resources, in consultation with
4 the Tidal Fisheries Advisory Commission, to identify by regulation based on certain
5 data the public clam fishery area in the Chesapeake Bay; prohibiting the lease of the
6 public clam fishery area for shellfish aquaculture; and generally relating to the
7 public clam fishery area in the Chesapeake Bay.
8 BY adding to
9 Article – Natural Resources
10 Section 4–1006.3
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
15 Article – Natural Resources
16 4–1006.3.
17 (A) IN CONSULTATION WITH THE TIDAL FISHERIES ADVISORY
18 COMMISSION, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL IDENTIFY BY REGULATION THE PUBLIC
19 CLAM FISHERY AREA IN THE CHESAPEAKE BAY BASED ON COMMERCIAL
20 HARVESTING ACTIVITY DURING THE 3 YEARS PRECEDING JUNE 1, 2025, ANY
21 SURVEYS CONDUCTED BY THE DEPARTMENT, AND OTHER QUANTITATIVE DATA
22 KNOWN OR MADE AVAILABLE TO THE DEPARTMENT.
23 (B) THE PUBLIC CLAM FISHERY AREA MAY NOT BE LEASED FOR SHELLFISH
24 AQUACULTURE.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *sb0841*
2 SENATE BILL 841
1 (C) A TIDAL FISH LICENSE HOLDER MAY CATCH CLAMS IN AREAS LOCATED
2 OUTSIDE THE PUBLIC CLAM FISHERY AREA UNLESS PROHIBITED BY LAW.
3 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect June
4 1, 2025.
