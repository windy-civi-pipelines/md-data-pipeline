J1 5lr2493
By: Delegates Alston, Acevero, Bagnall, Guzzone, Hill, Kaiser, Kaufman, Lopez,
Martinez, Miller, Taveras, Taylor, Tomlinson, White Holland, Woods, and
Woorman
Introduced and read first time: February 6, 2025
Assigned to: Health and Government Operations
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public Health – Prohibited Ingredients in Food
3 FOR the purpose of prohibiting a person from selling food that contains a certain dye in the
4 State unless the food packaging includes a certain warning; prohibiting a person
5 from manufacturing, selling, delivering, holding, or offering for sale in the State food
6 that contains certain ingredients; and generally relating to ingredients in food.
7 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
8 Article – Health – General
9 Section 21–101(a) and (i) and 21–1215
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
12 BY adding to
13 Article – Health – General
14 Section 21–259.4
15 Annotated Code of Maryland
16 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
17 BY repealing
18 Article – Health – General
19 Section 21–259.4
20 Annotated Code of Maryland
21 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
22 (As enacted by Section 1 of this Act)
23 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
24 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb1208*
2 HOUSE BILL 1208
1 Article – Health – General
2 21–101.
3 (a) In this title the following words have the meanings indicated.
4 (i) “Food” means:
5 (1) Any substance that is used as food or drink for human beings or as a
6 component of food or drink for human beings; or
7 (2) Chewing gum or any substance that is used as a component of chewing
8 gum.
9 21–259.4.
10 A PERSON MAY NOT SELL FOOD THAT CONTAINS RED DYE NO. 3 (CAS NO.
11 16423–68–0) IN THE STATE UNLESS THE FOOD PACKAGING INCLUDES A
12 CONSPICUOUS WARNING LABEL THAT STATES THE FOOD CONTAINS RED DYE NO. 3.
13 21–1215.
14 (a) This section does not apply to a violation of § 21–220(b)(4), § 21–228, or §
15 21–259.3 of this title.
16 (b) A person who violates any provision of Subtitle 2 of this title or any regulation
17 adopted under Subtitle 2 of this title is guilty of a misdemeanor and on conviction is subject
18 to:
19 (1) A fine not exceeding $10,000 or imprisonment not exceeding 1 year or
20 both; or
21 (2) If the person has been convicted once of violating Subtitle 2 of this title,
22 a fine not exceeding $25,000 or imprisonment not exceeding 3 years or both.
23 (c) In addition to any criminal penalties imposed under this section, a person who
24 violates any provision of Subtitle 2 of this title, any rule or regulation adopted under
25 Subtitle 2 of this title, or any term, condition, or limitation of any license or registration
26 issued under Subtitle 2 of this title:
27 (1) Is subject to a civil penalty not exceeding $5,000, in an action in any
28 District Court; and
29 (2) May be enjoined from continuing the violation.
30 (d) Each day on which a violation occurs is a separate violation under this section.
HOUSE BILL 1208 3
1 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That the Laws of Maryland read
2 as follows:
3 Article – Health – General
4 [21–259.4.
5 A person may not sell food that contains Red Dye No. 3 (CAS No. 16423–68–0) in the
6 State unless the food packaging includes a conspicuous warning label that states the food
7 contains Red Dye No. 3.]
8 21–259.4.
9 A PERSON MAY NOT MANUFACTURE, SELL, DELIVER, HOLD, OR OFFER FOR
10 SALE FOOD IN THE STATE THAT CONTAINS:
11 (1) BROMINATED VEGETABLE OIL (CAS NO. 8016–94–2);
12 (2) POTASSIUM BROMATE (CAS NO. 7758–01–2);
13 (3) PROPYLPARABEN (CAS NO. 94–13–3); OR
14 (4) RED DYE NO. 3 (CAS NO. 16423–68–0).
15 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That Section 2 of this Act shall take
16 effect October 1, 2028.
17 SECTION 4. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That, except as provided in Section
18 3 of this Act, this Act shall take effect October 1, 2025.
