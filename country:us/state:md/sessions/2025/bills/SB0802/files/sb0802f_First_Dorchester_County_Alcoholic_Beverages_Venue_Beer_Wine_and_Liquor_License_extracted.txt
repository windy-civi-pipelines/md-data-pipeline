Assigned to: Finance
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Dorchester County – Alcoholic Beverages – Venue Beer, Wine, and Liquor
3 License
4 FOR the purpose of establishing in Dorchester County a venue beer, wine, and liquor
5 license that authorizes the holder to sell or serve beer, wine, and liquor for
6 on–premises consumption during an event where food is served; authorizing the
7 Board of License Commissioners for Dorchester County to issue the license to a
8 nonprofit or for–profit retail business that hosts private or public events at a venue
9 that meets certain criteria; and generally relating to alcoholic beverages licenses in
10 Dorchester County.
11 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
12 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
13 Section 19–102
14 Annotated Code of Maryland
15 (2024 Replacement Volume)
16 BY adding to
17 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
18 Section 19–1001
19 Annotated Code of Maryland
20 (2024 Replacement Volume)
21 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
22 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
23 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
24 19–102.
25 This title applies only in Dorchester County.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *sb0802*
2 SENATE BILL 802
1 19–1001.
2 (A) THERE IS A VENUE BEER, WINE, AND LIQUOR LICENSE.
3 (B) THE BOARD MAY ISSUE THE LICENSE TO A NONPROFIT OR FOR–PROFIT
4 RETAIL BUSINESS THAT HOSTS WEDDINGS, BANQUETS, PARTIES, MEETINGS, AND
5 SIMILAR EVENTS, WHETHER PRIVATE OR OPEN TO THE PUBLIC, AT A VENUE THAT:
6 (1) IS AT LEAST 2,000 SQUARE FEET IN SIZE;
7 (2) CAN ACCOMMODATE AT LEAST 100 INDIVIDUALS FOR A SINGLE
8 EVENT, WHETHER INSIDE THE VENUE OR OUTSIDE ON THE VENUE’S PREMISES; AND
9 (3) HAS ADEQUATE BATHROOM FACILITIES.
10 (C) (1) THE LICENSE AUTHORIZES THE LICENSE HOLDER TO SELL OR
11 SERVE BEER, WINE, AND LIQUOR AT RETAIL, BY THE DRINK OR BY THE BOTTLE, FOR
12 ON–PREMISES CONSUMPTION:
13 (I) DURING AN EVENT HOSTED BY THE LICENSE HOLDER AT
14 THE LICENSE HOLDER’S VENUE; AND
15 (II) ONLY IF FOOD IS ALSO PROVIDED DURING THE EVENT.
16 (2) A LICENSE HOLDER MAY NOT ALLOW AN INDIVIDUAL TO CARRY
17 BEER, WINE, AND LIQUOR ONTO OR OFF THE LICENSED PREMISES.
18 (D) THE LICENSE MAY NOT BE TRANSFERRED TO ANOTHER LOCATION.
19 (E) THE LICENSE HOLDER MAY SELL BEER, WINE, AND LIQUOR DURING THE
20 HOURS AND DAYS AS SET OUT FOR A CLASS B BEER, WINE, AND LIQUOR LICENSE
21 UNDER § 19–2005 OF THIS TITLE.
22 (F) THE ANNUAL FEE FOR THE LICENSE IS $1,500.
23 (G) THE BOARD MAY ADOPT REGULATIONS TO CARRY OUT THIS SECTION.
24 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
25 1, 2025.
