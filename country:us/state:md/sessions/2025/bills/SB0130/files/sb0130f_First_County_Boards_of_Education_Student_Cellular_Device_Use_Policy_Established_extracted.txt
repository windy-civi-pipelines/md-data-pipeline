Section 1:
F1 5lr0757
(PRE–FILED)
By: Senator Benson
Requested: September 5, 2024
Introduced and read first time: January 8, 2025
Assigned to: Education, Energy, and the Environment
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 County Boards of Education – Student Cellular Device Use Policy – Established
3 FOR the purpose of requiring each county board of education to develop and implement a
4 policy that prohibits students from using a cellular device during the school day
5 except under certain circumstances; and generally relating to county boards of
6 education and student cellular device use policy.
7 BY adding to
8 Article – Education
9 Section 4–147
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
14 Article – Education
15 4–147.
16 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
17 INDICATED.
18 (2) “CELLULAR DEVICE” MEANS ANY PORTABLE, WIRELESS
19 COMMUNICATION DEVICE, INCLUDING A SMARTPHONE, TABLET, OR WEARABLE
20 DEVICE.
21 (3) “INSTRUCTIONAL TIME” MEANS ANY TIME DURING THE SCHOOL
22 DAY, EXCEPT A STUDENT’S LUNCH PERIOD.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *sb0130*
2 SENATE BILL 130
1 (B) EACH COUNTY BOARD SHALL DEVELOP AND IMPLEMENT A POLICY TO
2 PROHIBIT THE USE OF A CELLULAR DEVICE BY STUDENTS DURING INSTRUCTIONAL
3 TIME.
4 (C) EXCEPT AS PROVIDED IN SUBSECTION (D) OF THIS SECTION, THE
5 POLICY DEVELOPED UNDER THIS SECTION SHALL:
6 (1) PROHIBIT A STUDENT FROM USING A CELLULAR DEVICE DURING
7 INSTRUCTIONAL TIME; AND
8 (2) REQUIRE A STUDENT TO STORE THE STUDENT’S CELLULAR
9 DEVICE IN A SECURE STORAGE CONTAINER.
10 (D) THE POLICY DEVELOPED UNDER THIS SECTION MAY NOT PROHIBIT A
11 STUDENT FROM USING A CELLULAR DEVICE:
12 (1) FOR ANY PURPOSE DOCUMENTED IN A STUDENT’S
13 INDIVIDUALIZED EDUCATION PROGRAM OR SECTION 504 PLAN;
14 (2) TO MONITOR OR ADDRESS A STUDENT’S HEALTH ISSUE THAT HAS
15 BEEN DOCUMENTED BY A LICENSED MEDICAL PROFESSIONAL; OR
16 (3) FOR EDUCATION PURPOSES WHEN DIRECTED BY AN EDUCATOR
17 OR ADMINISTRATOR.
18 (E) THE POLICY DEVELOPED UNDER THIS SECTION SHALL INCLUDE
19 APPROPRIATE DISCIPLINARY MEASURES FOR VIOLATIONS OF THE POLICY.
20 (F) EACH COUNTY BOARD SHALL NOTIFY STUDENTS, PARENTS AND
21 GUARDIANS, AND SCHOOL PERSONNEL OF THE POLICY DEVELOPED AND
22 IMPLEMENTED UNDER THIS SECTION.
23 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
24 1, 2025.
