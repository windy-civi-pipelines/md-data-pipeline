A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 County Superintendent of Prince George’s County – Selection Process
3 – Alteration
4 PG 506–25
5 FOR the purpose of altering the process by which the County Superintendent of the Prince
6 George’s County public school system is selected by requiring the Prince George’s
7 County Board of Education to select the County Superintendent in a certain manner;
8 and generally relating to the selection process of the County Superintendent of the
9 Prince George’s County public school system.
10 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
11 Article – Education
12 Section 4–201.1
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
15 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
16 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
17 Article – Education
18 4–201.1.
19 (a) This section applies only in Prince George’s County.
20 (b) Subject to the provisions of subsection [(e)] (D) of this section, the County
21 Superintendent of the Prince George’s County public school system shall be[:

3 (2) Appointed] SELECTED by A MAJORITY OF THE MEMBERS OF the
4 county board FROM AMONG THE CANDIDATES CONSIDERED BY THE COUNTY BOARD
5 after agreement on contract terms negotiated by the chair of the county board.
6 [(c) (1) The County Executive shall select a County Superintendent from a list
7 of three nominees recommended by a search committee that is composed of:
8 (i) One member of the State Board, appointed by the State
9 Superintendent; and

20 Superintendent is a candidate for [reappointment] ANOTHER TERM.

24 before June 30.
25 (ii) If the [County Executive] COUNTY BOARD decides to select the
26 incumbent County Superintendent, the county board shall complete the [reappointment]
27 SELECTION no later than March 1 of that year.
28 (5) If the county board is unable to [appoint] SELECT a County
29 Superintendent by July 1 of the year a term begins, the provisions of subsection [(f)] (E) of
30 this section apply.
31 [(e) (1)] (D) An individual may not be [appointed] SELECTED as County
32 Superintendent unless the individual:

1 [(i) Is eligible to be issued a certificate for the office by the State
2 Superintendent;
3 (ii)] (1) Has graduated from an accredited college or university;
4 and
5 [(iii)] (2) Has completed 2 years of graduate work at an accredited
6 college or university, including public school administration, supervision, and methods of
7 teaching.
8 [(2) The appointment of the County Superintendent is not valid unless
9 approved in writing by the State Superintendent.
10 (3) If the State Superintendent disapproves an appointment, the State
11 Superintendent shall give the reasons for disapproval in writing to the county board and
12 the County Executive.]
13 [(f)] (E) If a vacancy occurs in the office of County Superintendent, [the County
14 Executive shall select and] the county board shall [appoint] SELECT an interim County
15 Superintendent to serve until July 1 after the [appointment] SELECTION.
16 [(g)] (F) On notification of pending criminal charges against the County
17 Superintendent as provided under § 4–206 of this subtitle, the county board may suspend
18 the County Superintendent with pay until the final disposition of the criminal charges.
19 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
20 1, 2025.
