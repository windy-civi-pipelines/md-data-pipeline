1 AN ACT concerning
2 Abandoned and Neglected Cemeteries Fund – Establishment and Income Tax
3 Checkoff
4 FOR the purpose of establishing the Abandoned and Neglected Cemeteries Fund;
5 establishing a certain income tax checkoff for voluntary contributions to the Fund;
6 requiring the Comptroller to include a checkoff on individual income tax return
7 forms for voluntary contributions to the Fund and to include certain information in
8 each individual income tax return package authorizing the Governor to include in
9 the annual budget bill a certain appropriation to the Fund; and generally relating to
10 the Abandoned and Neglected Cemeteries Fund.
11 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
12 Article – Business Regulation
13 Section 1–101(a), (f), and (h)

18 Section 5–805

20 (2024 Replacement Volume)
21 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
22 Article – State Finance and Procurement

25 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
26 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
27 Article – Business Regulation
28 1–101.
29 (a) In this article the following words have the meanings indicated.
30 (f) “Department” means the Maryland Department of Labor.
31 (h) “Secretary” means the Secretary of Labor.
32 5–805.
33 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
34 INDICATED.
SENATE BILL 963 3
1 (2) “ABANDONED CEMETERY” MEANS A CEMETERY FOR WHICH:
2 (I) 1. THE ORGANIZATION ESTABLISHED TO ENGAGE IN THE
3 OPERATION OF THE CEMETERY HAS BEEN TERMINATED, HAS BEEN SUBJECT TO
4 ADMINISTRATIVE DISSOLUTION BY THE STATE, OR HAS OTHERWISE CEASED TO
5 EXIST; AND
6 2. TITLE HAS NOT BEEN CONVEYED;
7 (II) THERE IS NO PROPERTY OWNER LISTED IN THE RECORDS OF
8 THE STATE DEPARTMENT OF ASSESSMENTS AND TAXATION;
9 (III) THE PROPERTY HAS BEEN CONDEMNED; OR
10 (IV) NO PERSON WHO IS LEGALLY RESPONSIBLE FOR THE
11 PROPERTY CAN BE FOUND AND LEGAL OWNERSHIP OF THE PROPERTY CANNOT BE
12 DETERMINED.
13 (3) “FUND” MEANS THE ABANDONED AND NEGLECTED CEMETERIES
14 FUND.
15 (4) “NEGLECTED CEMETERY” MEANS A CEMETERY FOR WHICH:
16 (I) TWO OR MORE CITATIONS HAVE BEEN ISSUED BY A COUNTY
17 OR MUNICIPAL CORPORATION AGAINST THE PROPERTY FOR FAILURE TO MAINTAIN
18 THE PROPERTY;
19 (II) THE PROPERTY HAS FALLEN INTO DISREPAIR DUE TO
20 NEGLECT AND INSUFFICIENT MAINTENANCE;
21 (III) THE PROPERTY HAS BEEN THE OBJECT OF VANDALISM,
22 LOITERING, OR OTHER CRIMINAL CONDUCT; OR
23 (IV) THERE HAS BEEN PHYSICAL DESTRUCTION OR
24 DETERIORATION OF THE PROPERTY.
25 (B) THERE IS AN ABANDONED AND NEGLECTED CEMETERIES FUND.
26 (C) THE PURPOSE OF THE FUND IS TO PROVIDE FOR THE CARE,
27 PRESERVATION, MAINTENANCE, AND RESTORATION OF ABANDONED AND
28 NEGLECTED CEMETERIES IN THE STATE.
29 (D) THE SECRETARY SHALL ADMINISTER THE FUND.
4 SENATE BILL 963
1 (E) (1) THE FUND IS A SPECIAL, NONLAPSING FUND THAT IS NOT
2 SUBJECT TO § 7–302 OF THE STATE FINANCE AND PROCUREMENT ARTICLE.
3 (2) THE STATE TREASURER SHALL HOLD THE FUND SEPARATELY,
4 AND THE COMPTROLLER SHALL ACCOUNT FOR THE FUND.
5 (F) THE FUND CONSISTS OF:
6 (1) THE NET PROCEEDS FROM CONTRIBUTIONS UNDER THE INCOME
7 TAX CHECKOFF ESTABLISHED UNDER § 2–120 OF THE TAX – GENERAL ARTICLE;
8 (2) MONEY APPROPRIATED IN THE STATE BUDGET TO THE FUND;
9 (3) (2) INTEREST EARNINGS; AND
10 (4) (3) ANY OTHER MONEY FROM ANY OTHER SOURCE ACCEPTED
11 FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE FUND.
12 (G) (1) MONEY IN THE FUND MAY BE USED ONLY FOR THE COSTS OF:
13 (I) CARE, PRESERVATION, MAINTENANCE, AND RESTORATION
14 OF ABANDONED AND NEGLECTED CEMETERIES;
15 (II) ADMINISTERING THE FUND THROUGH DISTRIBUTION TO AN
16 ADMINISTRATIVE COST ACCOUNT IN THE DEPARTMENT; AND
17 (III) SUBJECT TO PARAGRAPH (2) OF THIS SUBSECTION,
18 PROMOTING DONATIONS TO THE FUND THROUGH DISTRIBUTION TO A PROMOTION
19 COST ACCOUNT IN THE DEPARTMENT.
20 (2) NOT MORE THAN 5% OF THE NET PROCEEDS OF THE FUND MAY
21 BE USED UNDER PARAGRAPH (1)(III) OF THIS SUBSECTION.
22 (H) (1) THE STATE TREASURER SHALL INVEST THE MONEY OF THE FUND
23 IN THE SAME MANNER AS OTHER STATE MONEY MAY BE INVESTED.
24 (2) ANY INTEREST EARNINGS OF THE FUND SHALL BE CREDITED TO
25 THE FUND.
26 (I) EXPENDITURES FROM THE FUND MAY BE MADE ONLY IN ACCORDANCE
27 WITH THE STATE BUDGET.
28 (J) MONEY EXPENDED FROM THE FUND FOR THE PRESERVATION, CARE,
29 AND MAINTENANCE OF ABANDONED AND NEGLECTED CEMETERIES IS

1 SUPPLEMENTAL TO AND IS NOT INTENDED TO TAKE THE PLACE OF FUNDING THAT
2 OTHERWISE WOULD BE APPROPRIATED FOR THE OFFICE OF CEMETERY
3 OVERSIGHT.
4 (K) THE GOVERNOR MAY INCLUDE IN THE ANNUAL BUDGET BILL AN
5 APPROPRIATION OF $250,000 TO THE FUND.
6 (K) (L) ON OR BEFORE SEPTEMBER 30 EACH YEAR, THE SECRETARY
7 SHALL SUBMIT A REPORT TO THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY, IN ACCORDANCE WITH §
8 2–1257 OF THE STATE GOVERNMENT ARTICLE, ON THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE
9 FUND.
10 (L) (M) THE REPORT REQUIRED UNDER SUBSECTION (K) (L) OF THIS
11 SECTION SHALL INCLUDE:
12 (1) THE GROSS AMOUNT OF DONATIONS TO THE FUND;
13 (2) THE COSTS OF ADMINISTRATION BY THE COMPTROLLER OF THE
14 INCOME TAX CHECKOFF SYSTEM;
15 (3) A DESCRIPTION OF PROMOTIONAL EFFORTS UNDERTAKEN WITH
16 MONEY FROM THE FUND; AND
17 (4) (3) A DETAILED ACCOUNTING OF THE USE OF THE FUND.

27 Fund of the State.

8 2024 through 2028, net interest on all State money allocated by the State Treasurer under

16 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That the Laws of Maryland read
17 as follows:
18 Article – Tax – General
19 2–120.
20 (A) (1) THE COMPTROLLER SHALL INCLUDE ON THE INDIVIDUAL
21 INCOME TAX RETURN FORM A CHECKOFF DESIGNATED AS THE “ABANDONED AND
22 NEGLECTED CEMETERIES FUND”.
23 (2) THE CHECKOFF SHALL STATE THAT:
24 (I) THE INDIVIDUAL, OR EACH SPOUSE IN THE CASE OF A JOINT
25 RETURN, MAY CONTRIBUTE TO THE ABANDONED AND NEGLECTED CEMETERIES
26 FUND THE AMOUNT DESIGNATED BY THE INDIVIDUAL; AND
27 (II) 1. THE INDIVIDUAL SHALL DEDUCT THE AMOUNT OF THE
28 CONTRIBUTION FROM ANY REFUND TO WHICH THE INDIVIDUAL IS ENTITLED; OR
29 2. IF THE INDIVIDUAL IS NOT ENTITLED TO A REFUND,
30 THE INDIVIDUAL SHALL ADD THE AMOUNT OF THE CONTRIBUTION TO THE INCOME
31 TAX TO BE PAID WITH THE RETURN.
SENATE BILL 963 7
1 (3) THE COMPTROLLER SHALL INCLUDE, WITH THE INDIVIDUAL
2 INCOME TAX RETURN PACKAGE, A DESCRIPTION OF THE PURPOSES FOR WHICH THE
3 ABANDONED AND NEGLECTED CEMETERIES FUND WAS ESTABLISHED AND THE
4 PURPOSES FOR WHICH THE FUND MAY BE USED.
5 (B) THE COMPTROLLER SHALL:
6 (1) COLLECT THE CHECKOFF CONTRIBUTIONS AND ACCOUNT TO THE
7 STATE TREASURER FOR THE MONEY COLLECTED;
8 (2) FROM THE CONTRIBUTIONS COLLECTED, DISTRIBUTE THE
9 AMOUNT NECESSARY TO ADMINISTER THE CHECKOFF TO AN ADMINISTRATIVE COST
10 ACCOUNT; AND
11 (3) AFTER THE DISTRIBUTION UNDER ITEM (2) OF THIS SUBSECTION,
12 DISTRIBUTE THE REMAINDER OF THE MONEY COLLECTED UNDER THIS SUBSECTION
13 TO THE ABANDONED AND NEGLECTED CEMETERIES FUND ESTABLISHED UNDER §
14 5–805 OF THE BUSINESS REGULATION ARTICLE.
15 10–804.
16 (M) AN INDIVIDUAL MAY DESIGNATE A CONTRIBUTION TO THE ABANDONED
17 AND NEGLECTED CEMETERIES FUND ESTABLISHED UNDER § 5–805 OF THE
18 BUSINESS REGULATION ARTICLE BY THE CHECKOFF ON THE RETURN.
19 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That Section 2 of this Act shall take
20 effect July 1, 2026, and shall be applicable to all taxable years beginning after December
21 31, 2025.
22 SECTION 4. 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That, except as provided in
23 Section 3 of this Act, this Act shall take effect October 1, 2025.
