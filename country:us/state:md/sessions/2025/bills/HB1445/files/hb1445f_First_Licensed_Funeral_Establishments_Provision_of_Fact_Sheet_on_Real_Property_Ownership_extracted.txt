Introduced and read first time: February 7, 2025
Assigned to: Judiciary
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Licensed Funeral Establishments – Provision of Fact Sheet on Real Property
3 Ownership
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Department of Housing and Community Development to
5 develop and publish a fact sheet about the legal issues and resources related to the
6 ownership of residential real property following the death of a homeowner; requiring
7 a licensed funeral establishment to provide a printed copy of the fact sheet to certain
8 individuals during the course of providing funeral services; and generally relating to
9 licensed funeral establishments and the ownership of residential real property
10 following the death of a homeowner.
11 BY adding to
12 Article – Health Occupations
13 Section 7–407.1
14 Annotated Code of Maryland
15 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
16 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
17 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
18 Article – Health Occupations
19 7–407.1.
20 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “IMMEDIATE FAMILY MEMBER” MEANS A SPOUSE,
21 CHILD, SIBLING, PARENT, GRANDPARENT, GRANDCHILD, STEPPARENT, STEPCHILD,
22 AND STEPSIBLING.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb1445*
2 HOUSE BILL 1445
1 (B) (1) THE DEPARTMENT OF HOUSING AND COMMUNITY
2 DEVELOPMENT SHALL DEVELOP AND PUBLISH ON ITS WEBSITE A FACT SHEET THAT
3 PROVIDES INFORMATION ABOUT:
4 (I) THE LEGAL ISSUES RELATED TO DEEDS, HEIRS, AND THE
5 OWNERSHIP OF RESIDENTIAL REAL PROPERTY FOLLOWING THE DEATH OF A
6 HOMEOWNER, COMMONLY KNOWN AS A TANGLED TITLE;
7 (II) HOW AN INDIVIDUAL CAN ADDRESS THE LEGAL ISSUES IN
8 ITEM (I) OF THIS PARAGRAPH; AND
9 (III) THE LEGAL RESOURCES AND FREE OR LOW–COST SERVICES
10 THAT ARE AVAILABLE TO LOW–INCOME INDIVIDUALS IN THE AREAS OF PROBATE
11 LAW AND ESTATE ADMINISTRATION.
12 (2) THE DEPARTMENT OF HOUSING AND COMMUNITY
13 DEVELOPMENT SHALL:
14 (I) PROVIDE THE FACT SHEET IN A PRINTABLE FORMAT FROM
15 ITS WEBSITE; AND
16 (II) AS NEEDED, UPDATE THE FACT SHEET.
17 (C) DURING THE COURSE OF PROVIDING FUNERAL SERVICES TO A
18 CONSUMER, A LICENSED FUNERAL ESTABLISHMENT SHALL PROVIDE A PRINTED
19 COPY OF THE FACT SHEET PUBLISHED UNDER SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION TO
20 THE IMMEDIATE FAMILY MEMBERS OF THE DECEASED OR THE AUTHORIZED
21 REPRESENTATIVE.
22 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect June
23 1, 2025.
