By: Delegate Mangione
Requested: September 13, 2024
Introduced and read first time: January 8, 2025
Assigned to: Environment and Transportation
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Workgroup to Study School Bus Safety
3 (Maryland School Bus Safety Act of 2025)
4 FOR the purpose of establishing the Workgroup to Study School Bus Safety; and generally
5 relating to the Workgroup to Study School Bus Safety.
6 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
7 That:
8 (a) There is a Workgroup to Study School Bus Safety.
9 (b) The Governor shall appoint members of the Workgroup.
10 (c) The Governor shall designate the chair of the Workgroup.
11 (d) The Maryland Department of Transportation, in consultation with the State
12 Department of Education, shall provide staff for the Workgroup.
13 (e) A member of the Workgroup:
14 (1) may not receive compensation as a member of the Workgroup; but
15 (2) is entitled to reimbursement for expenses under the Standard State
16 Travel Regulations, as provided in the State budget.
17 (f) The Workgroup shall study and identify issues related to safety for and
18 behavior of students and bus drivers on buses driven to or from a primary or secondary
19 school in the State.
20 (g) On or before June 30, 2026, the Workgroup shall report its findings and

5 1, 2025. It shall remain effective for a period of 2 years and, at the end of June 30, 2027,
