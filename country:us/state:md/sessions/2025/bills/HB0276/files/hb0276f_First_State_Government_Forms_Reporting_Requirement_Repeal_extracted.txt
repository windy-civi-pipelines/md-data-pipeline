Section 1:
P3 5lr0326
(PRE–FILED) CF SB 260
By: Chair, Health and Government Operations Committee (By Request –
Departmental – General Services)
Requested: September 19, 2024
Introduced and read first time: January 8, 2025
Assigned to: Health and Government Operations
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 State Government – Forms – Reporting Requirement – Repeal
3 FOR the purpose of repealing a requirement that certain departments or units of State
4 government annually submit a certain report regarding forms to the Department of
5 General Services; and generally relating to forms.
6 BY repealing
7 Article – State Government
8 Section 10–605
9 Annotated Code of Maryland
10 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
11 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
12 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
13 Article – State Government
14 [10–605.
15 On or before July 31 of each year, each department or independent unit shall submit
16 to the Division an annual report on its activities as to the management of its forms during
17 the previous fiscal year.]
18 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
19 October 1, 2025.
