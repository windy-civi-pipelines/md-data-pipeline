A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Vehicle Registration – Fee Exemptions – Military Honorees
3 FOR the purpose of exempting recipients of the Purple Heart, the Medal of Honor, the
4 Silver Star, or the Bronze Star and members of Gold Star families from the
5 requirement to pay vehicle registration fees; and generally relating to vehicle
6 registration fee exemptions.
7 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
8 Article – Transportation
9 Section 13–903(a)
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
14 Article – Transportation
15 13–903.
16 (a) The following vehicles are exempt from the registration fees specified in this
17 subtitle:
18 (1) A vehicle that is owned and operated by the United States, this State,
19 or any political subdivision of this State;
20 (2) A vehicle that is owned by a volunteer fire company incorporated in this
21 State or by a rescue squad and that is used for fire fighting or ambulance purposes;
22 (3) A canteen wagon of a recognized fire buff organization, as certified by
