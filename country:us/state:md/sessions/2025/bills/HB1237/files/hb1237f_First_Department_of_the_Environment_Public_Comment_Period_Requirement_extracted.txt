By: Delegate Feldmark
Introduced and read first time: February 7, 2025
Assigned to: Environment and Transportation
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Department of the Environment – Public Comment Period – Requirement
3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Department of the Environment to accept public
4 comments on certain applications during a certain time period after the date on
5 which notice of the application is published in a newspaper of general circulation;
6 and generally relating to public comment periods.
7 BY adding to
8 Article – Environment
9 Section 1–206
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2013 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
14 Article – Environment
15 1–206.
16 WHENEVER THIS ARTICLE REQUIRES NOTICE OF AN APPLICATION FOR A
17 PERMIT, LICENSE, OR OTHER AUTHORIZATION TO BE PUBLISHED IN A NEWSPAPER
18 OF GENERAL CIRCULATION, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL ACCEPT PUBLIC COMMENTS
19 ON THE APPLICATION FOR:
20 (1) THE TIME PERIOD SPECIFIED IN STATUTE OR REGULATION; OR
21 (2) IF NO TIME PERIOD IS SPECIFIED IN STATUTE OR REGULATION, AT
22 LEAST 14 DAYS AFTER THE DATE OF PUBLICATION.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb1237*
2 HOUSE BILL 1237
1 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
2 October 1, 2025.
