A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Anne Arundel County Board of Education – Student Member Scholarship –
3 Increase
4 FOR the purpose of increasing the scholarship award for a student member of the Anne
5 Arundel County Board of Education who completes a full term on the county board;
6 and generally relating to the scholarship for the student member of the Anne
7 Arundel County Board of Education.
8 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
9 Article – Education
10 Section 3–2A–01(a)
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
13 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
14 Article – Education
15 Section 3–2A–07
16 Annotated Code of Maryland
17 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
18 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
19 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
20 Article – Education
21 3–2A–01.
22 (a) The Anne Arundel County Board of Education consists of:
23 (1) Seven nonpartisan elected members; and
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb0402*
2 HOUSE BILL 402
1 (2) One student member.
2 3–2A–07.
3 (a) The president of the county board is entitled to receive $17,000 annually as
4 compensation, the vice president is entitled to receive $16,000 annually as compensation,
5 and, except for the student member, the other board members are entitled to receive
6 $15,000 each annually as compensation.
7 (b) A student member who completes a full term on the county board shall be
8 granted a scholarship of [$8,000] $15,000 to be applied toward the student’s higher
9 education costs.
10 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect June
11 1, 2025.
