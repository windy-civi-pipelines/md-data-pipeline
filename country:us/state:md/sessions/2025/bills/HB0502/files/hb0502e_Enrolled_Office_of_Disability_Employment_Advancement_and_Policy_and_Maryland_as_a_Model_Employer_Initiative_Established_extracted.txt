1 AN ACT concerning
2 Office of Disability Employment Advancement and Policy and Maryland as a
3 Model Employer Initiative – Established
4 FOR the purpose of establishing the Office of Disability Employment Advancement and
5 Policy within the Department of Disabilities; establishing the Maryland as a Model
6 Employer Initiative within the Office of Disability Employment Advancement and
7 Policy to facilitate efforts that improve outcomes in the hiring, recruitment,
8 retention, and advancement of people with disabilities in the State government
9 workforce; and generally relating to the establishment of the Office of Disability
10 Employment Advancement and Policy and the Maryland as a Model Employer
11 Initiative.

5 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)

12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
14 Article – Human Services
15 7–114.
16 (b) The Department shall oversee and administer the following programs and
17 units:
18 (1) constituent services and ombudsmen programs;
19 (2) the Assistive Technology Guaranteed Loan Program under Subtitle 6
20 of this title;
21 (3) the Office of Personal Assistance Services, including the Attendant
22 Care Program under Subtitle 4 of this title;
23 (4) Telecommunications Access of Maryland under Subtitle 8 of this title;
24 [and]
25 (5) Telecommunications Devices and Distribution of Accessible
26 Information for Disabled Individuals under Subtitle 9 of this title; AND
27 (6) THE OFFICE OF DISABILITY EMPLOYMENT ADVANCEMENT AND
28 POLICY.
29 SUBTITLE 12. OFFICE OF DISABILITY EMPLOYMENT ADVANCEMENT AND POLICY.
30 7–1201.
31 (A) IN THIS SUBTITLE THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
32 INDICATED.
HOUSE BILL 502 3
1 (B) “DEPARTMENT” MEANS THE DEPARTMENT OF DISABILITIES.
2 (C) “INITIATIVE” MEANS THE MARYLAND AS A MODEL EMPLOYER
3 INITIATIVE.
4 (D) “OFFICE” MEANS THE OFFICE OF DISABILITY EMPLOYMENT
5 ADVANCEMENT AND POLICY.
6 (E) “SECRETARY” MEANS THE SECRETARY OF DISABILITIES.
7 7–1202.
8 (A) THERE IS AN OFFICE OF DISABILITY EMPLOYMENT ADVANCEMENT
9 AND POLICY WITHIN THE DEPARTMENT.
10 (B) THE PURPOSE OF THE OFFICE INCLUDES:
11 (1) THE DEVELOPMENT AND IMPLEMENTATION OF STRATEGIES IN
12 THE STATE DISABILITIES PLAN, ESTABLISHED UNDER § 7–132 OF THIS TITLE,
13 RELATING TO THE EMPLOYMENT, TRAINING, AND CAREER–READINESS OF
14 INDIVIDUALS WITH DISABILITIES;
15 (2) PERFORMING DUTIES UNDER § 7–114 OF THIS TITLE NECESSARY
16 TO SUPPORT STATEWIDE POLICIES AND STANDARDS RELATING TO THE
17 EMPLOYMENT, TRAINING, AND CAREER–READINESS OF INDIVIDUALS WITH
18 DISABILITIES;
19 (3) COLLECTING INFORMATION AND DATA FROM UNITS OF STATE
20 GOVERNMENT RELATING TO THE EMPLOYMENT, TRAINING, AND
21 CAREER–READINESS OF INDIVIDUALS WITH DISABILITIES, IN ACCORDANCE WITH §
22 7–115 OF THIS TITLE; AND
23 (4) IMPLEMENTING THE MARYLAND AS A MODEL EMPLOYER
24 INITIATIVE, ESTABLISHED UNDER § 7–1205 OF THIS SUBTITLE.
25 (C) BEGINNING IN FISCAL YEAR 2027, THE GOVERNOR MAY INCLUDE IN THE
26 ANNUAL BUDGET BILL AN APPROPRIATION OF $250,000 FOR THE OFFICE.
27 7–1203.
28 THE SECRETARY MAY ADOPT REGULATIONS AS NECESSARY TO CARRY OUT
29 THE PROVISIONS OF THIS SUBTITLE.
4 HOUSE BILL 502
1 7–1204.
2 THIS SUBTITLE MAY NOT BE CONSTRUED TO ESTABLISH AN ENTITLEMENT
3 PROGRAM.
4 7–1205.
5 (A) BEGINNING JULY 1, 2026, THE OFFICE SHALL BE RESPONSIBLE FOR
6 IMPLEMENTATION AND OVERSIGHT OF THE MARYLAND AS A MODEL EMPLOYER
7 INITIATIVE.
8 (B) THE PURPOSE OF THE INITIATIVE SHALL INCLUDE:
9 (1) PROMOTING THE RECRUITMENT, HIRING, RETENTION, AND
10 CAREER ADVANCEMENT OF PEOPLE WITH DISABILITIES ACROSS STATE
11 GOVERNMENT;
12 (2) CONDUCTING AND COORDINATING OUTREACH TO JOB SEEKERS
13 WITH DISABILITIES AND STATE GOVERNMENT TO ENCOURAGE PARTICIPATION IN
14 THE INITIATIVE;
15 (3) DEVELOPING AND DELIVERING TRAININGS TO STATE AGENCIES
16 AND UNITS ADDRESSING BEST PRACTICES, RESOURCES, AND RELEVANT FEDERAL
17 AND STATE LAWS PERTAINING TO THE EMPLOYMENT OF PEOPLE WITH
18 DISABILITIES;
19 (4) ASSISTING STATE GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND UNITS TO
20 ATTRACT QUALIFIED JOB SEEKERS WITH DISABILITIES TO AVAILABLE POSITIONS;
21 (5) PROMOTING ACCESS TO AND ACQUISITION OF ASSISTIVE
22 TECHNOLOGY FOR NEW STATE EMPLOYEES WITH DISABILITIES;
23 (6) EVALUATING STATE EMPLOYMENT PRACTICES AND POLICIES AND
24 MAKING RECOMMENDATIONS REGARDING WAYS TO REDUCE BARRIERS FOR JOB
25 SEEKERS AND EMPLOYEES WITH DISABILITIES;
26 (7) COLLECTING FROM EACH UNIT OF STATE GOVERNMENT, AS PART
27 OF THE PLANS REQUIRED UNDER § 7–116 OF THIS TITLE, PERFORMANCE GOALS
28 RELATED TO HIRING AND RETENTION EFFORTS FOR EMPLOYEES WITH DISABILITIES
29 AND ANY DATA DEEMED NECESSARY BY THE SECRETARY TO DEMONSTRATE
30 PERFORMANCE OUTCOMES; AND
31 (8) REPORTING ON THE INITIATIVE’S OUTCOMES IN COMPLIANCE
32 WITH SUBSECTION (C) OF THIS SECTION.
HOUSE BILL 502 5
1 (C) ON OR BEFORE DECEMBER 1, 2026, AND EACH DECEMBER 1
2 THEREAFTER, THE OFFICE SHALL REPORT TO THE GOVERNOR AND THE GENERAL
3 ASSEMBLY, IN ACCORDANCE WITH § 2–1257 OF THE STATE GOVERNMENT ARTICLE,
4 ON:
5 (1) PROGRESS AND OUTCOMES OF THE INITIATIVE; AND
6 (2) RECOMMENDATIONS DEVELOPED BY THE INITIATIVE FOR
7 CONTINUED IMPROVEMENT OF STATE GOVERNMENT EMPLOYMENT PRACTICES FOR
8 PEOPLE WITH DISABILITIES.
9 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
10 1, 2025.
Approved:

Governor.
