By: Delegate Valentine
Requested: October 2, 2024
Introduced and read first time: January 8, 2025
Assigned to: Judiciary
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Department of Public Safety and Correctional Services – Study on Location of
3 Individuals Prior to and Following Incarceration
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Department of Public Safety and Correctional Services
5 to conduct a certain study examining certain data regarding an individual’s
6 residence prior to incarceration and on release from a State correctional facility; and
7 generally relating to State correctional facilities.
8 BY adding to
9 Article – Correctional Services
10 Section 9–618
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2017 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
15 Article – Correctional Services
16 9–618.
17 (A) ON OR BEFORE OCTOBER 1, 2026, AND EACH OCTOBER 1 THEREAFTER,
18 THE DEPARTMENT SHALL CONDUCT A STUDY ON THE LOCATION OF INDIVIDUALS
19 BEFORE AND FOLLOWING INCARCERATION IN A STATE CORRECTIONAL FACILITY
20 AND REPORT THE RESULTS OF THE STUDY TO THE SENATE JUDICIAL PROCEEDINGS
21 COMMITTEE, THE SENATE BUDGET AND TAXATION COMMITTEE, THE HOUSE
22 JUDICIARY COMMITTEE, AND THE HOUSE APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE, IN
23 ACCORDANCE WITH § 2–1257 OF THE STATE GOVERNMENT ARTICLE.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb0022*
2 HOUSE BILL 22
1 (B) THE STUDY SHALL INCLUDE:
2 (1) DATA ON THE COUNTY OF RESIDENCE OF EACH INDIVIDUAL
3 RELEASED DURING THE PREVIOUS YEAR, AS REPORTED BY THE INDIVIDUAL ON
4 INTAKE TO THE FACILITY;
5 (2) DATA ON THE COUNTY OF RELEASE FOLLOWING INCARCERATION;
6 (3) FOR INDIVIDUALS WHO WERE TRANSFERRED FROM ONE STATE
7 CORRECTIONAL FACILITY TO ANOTHER PRIOR TO RELEASE, THE NUMBER OF DAYS
8 THAT THE TRANSFER OCCURRED BEFORE RELEASE; AND
9 (4) A COMPARISON OF:
10 (I) THE NUMBER OF INDIVIDUALS RELEASED FROM A STATE
11 CORRECTIONAL FACILITY INTO THE SAME COUNTY IN WHICH THEY RESIDED PRIOR
12 TO INCARCERATION;
13 (II) THE NUMBER OF INDIVIDUALS RELEASED FROM A STATE
14 CORRECTIONAL FACILITY INTO THE SAME COUNTY IN WHICH CONVICTION
15 OCCURRED; AND
16 (III) THE NUMBER OF INDIVIDUALS RELEASED FROM A STATE
17 CORRECTIONAL FACILITY INTO THE COUNTY IN WHICH THEY WERE INCARCERATED
18 AT THE TIME OF RELEASE.
19 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
20 October 1, 2025.
