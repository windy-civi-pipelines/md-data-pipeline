CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Business Regulation – Cemeteries – Requirements for Sale or Transfer or
3 Government Acquisition
4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting certain owners of a cemetery from selling or transferring
5 the cemetery without the approval of the Director of the Office of Cemetery
6 Oversight; exempting a certain cemetery owner from the requirement to obtain an
7 approval from the Director; authorizing a governmental unit to acquire an
8 abandoned cemetery under certain circumstances; establishing certain requirements
9 for the sale, transfer, or government acquisition of a cemetery; and generally relating
10 to the sale, transfer, or acquisition of cemeteries.
11 BY repealing
12 Article – Business Regulation
13 Section 5–505
14 Annotated Code of Maryland
15 (2024 Replacement Volume)
16 BY adding to
17 Article – Business Regulation
18 Section 5–505 and 5–507
19 Annotated Code of Maryland
20 (2024 Replacement Volume)

3 Article – Business Regulation
4 [5–505.
5 (a) An action may be brought in accordance with the Maryland Rules and a court
6 may pass a judgment for sale of a burial ground for another purpose if:
7 (1) the ground has been dedicated and used for burial;
8 (2) burial lots have been sold in the burial ground and deeds executed or
9 certificates issued to buyers of the lots;
10 (3) the ground has ceased to be used for burial; and
11 (4) it is desirable to dispose of the burial ground for another purpose.
12 (b) If the court is satisfied that it is expedient or would be in the interest of the
13 parties to sell the burial ground, the court:
14 (1) may pass a judgment for the sale of the burial ground on the terms and
15 notice the court sets;
16 (2) shall order that as much of the proceeds of the sale as necessary be used
17 to pay the expenses of removing any human remains in the burial ground, buying burial
18 lots in another burial ground, and reburying the remains; and
19 (3) shall distribute the remaining proceeds of the sale among the parties
20 according to their interests.
21 (c) A judgment for the sale of a burial ground passes to the buyer of the burial
22 ground the title to the burial ground free of the claims of:
23 (1) the owners of the burial ground; and
24 (2) the holders of burial lots.]
