1 AN ACT concerning
2 Real Property – Insufficient Condominium Reserve Account Grant Fund –
3 Establishment
4 FOR the purpose of requiring certain regulated financial institutions to develop a
5 community benefit plan in connection with certain applications to the Commissioner
6 of Financial Regulation relating to financial services; requiring certain regulated
7 financial institutions to submit to the Commissioner and make available to the
8 public a report on community benefit goal attainment under certain circumstances;
9 requiring the Commissioner to exercise certain oversight authority relating to the
10 adequacy of the community benefit plans and attainment of goals under the plans;
11 requiring the Commissioner to establish a certain community benefit review
12 committee; establishing the Community Reinvestment Fund as a special, nonlapsing
13 fund to finance certain community development activities; requiring the
14 Commissioner to contract with certain persons to conduct a certain disparity study;
15 and generally relating to financial institutions and community development.
