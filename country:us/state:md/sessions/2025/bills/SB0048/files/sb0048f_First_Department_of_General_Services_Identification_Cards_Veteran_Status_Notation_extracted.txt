Section 1:
P3, P1 5lr0615
(PRE–FILED)
By: Senators Simonaire and Jackson
Requested: May 18, 2024
Introduced and read first time: January 8, 2025
Assigned to: Education, Energy, and the Environment
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Department of General Services – Identification Cards – Veteran Status
3 Notation
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing the Department of General Services to include a notation
5 on a certain identification card indicating that the holder is a veteran under certain
6 circumstances; requiring the Department to obtain the consent of a certain holder of
7 an identification card before transmitting certain information to a certain executive
8 agency; prohibiting the Department from transmitting certain information obtained
9 on an application for an identification card except under certain circumstances; and
10 generally relating to veterans and identification cards issued by the Department of
11 General Services.
12 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
13 Article – State Finance and Procurement
14 Section 4–101
15 Annotated Code of Maryland
16 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
17 BY adding to
18 Article – State Finance and Procurement
19 Section 4–211
20 Annotated Code of Maryland
21 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
22 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
23 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
24 Article – State Finance and Procurement
25 4–101.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.

2 SENATE BILL 48
1 (a) In this title the following words have the meanings indicated.
2 (b) “Department” means the Department of General Services.
3 (c) “Secretary” means the Secretary of General Services.
4 4–211.
5 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
6 INDICATED.
7 (2) “EXECUTIVE AGENCY” MEANS A UNIT OF THE EXECUTIVE
8 BRANCH OF STATE GOVERNMENT.
9 (3) “IDENTIFICATION CARD” MEANS A SECURITY CARD ISSUED BY
10 THE DEPARTMENT TO A STATE EMPLOYEE OR OTHER INDIVIDUAL AUTHORIZED BY
11 THE SECRETARY, INCLUDING A CONTRACTOR, REGULATED LOBBYIST, MEDIA
12 PERSONNEL, LOCAL GOVERNMENT OFFICIAL, OR VOLUNTEER.
13 (B) (1) THE DEPARTMENT MAY INCLUDE A NOTATION ON AN
14 IDENTIFICATION CARD INDICATING THAT THE HOLDER IS A VETERAN IF THE
15 INDIVIDUAL ISSUED THE IDENTIFICATION CARD PRESENTS A CERTIFICATION OF
16 VETERAN STATUS OBTAINED FROM THE DEPARTMENT OF VETERANS AND MILITARY
17 FAMILIES IN ACCORDANCE WITH § 9–905 OF THE STATE GOVERNMENT ARTICLE, A
18 DD FORM 214, OR OTHER DOCUMENTATION ACCEPTABLE TO THE DEPARTMENT.
19 (2) THE DEPARTMENT MAY LIMIT THE NOTATION AUTHORIZED BY
20 PARAGRAPH (1) OF THIS SUBSECTION TO A CERTAIN CATEGORY OR CATEGORIES OF
21 IDENTIFICATION CARD HOLDERS.
22 (C) (1) IF THE DEPARTMENT PROVIDES AN OPTION FOR A NOTATION ON
23 AN IDENTIFICATION CARD THAT INDICATES THAT THE HOLDER IS A VETERAN, THE
24 DEPARTMENT SHALL INCLUDE ON THE APPLICATION FOR THE IDENTIFICATION
25 CARD THE OPTION FOR THE APPLICANT TO:
26 (I) INDICATE THAT THE APPLICANT IS A VETERAN; AND
27 (II) CONSENT TO BEING CONTACTED BY APPROPRIATE
28 EXECUTIVE AGENCIES REGARDING THE INDIVIDUAL’S ELIGIBILITY FOR STATE OR
29 FEDERAL VETERANS’ BENEFITS.
30 (2) (I) IN ACCORDANCE WITH TITLE 4, SUBTITLE 3 OF THE
31 GENERAL PROVISIONS ARTICLE AND ANY OTHER APPLICABLE LAW, AND ON
SENATE BILL 48 3
1 REQUEST BY AN EXECUTIVE AGENCY, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL TRANSMIT TO THE
2 EXECUTIVE AGENCY APPROPRIATE INFORMATION ABOUT EACH IDENTIFICATION
3 CARD HOLDER WHO HAS GIVEN CONSENT UNDER PARAGRAPH (1)(II) OF THIS
4 SUBSECTION.
5 (II) UNLESS OTHERWISE AUTHORIZED OR REQUIRED BY LAW,
6 THE DEPARTMENT MAY NOT TRANSMIT INFORMATION OBTAINED ON AN
7 APPLICATION FOR AN IDENTIFICATION CARD THAT RELATES TO THE APPLICANT’S
8 STATUS AS A VETERAN UNLESS THE APPLICANT HAS GIVEN CONSENT UNDER
9 PARAGRAPH (1)(II) OF THIS SUBSECTION.
10 (D) THE DEPARTMENT MAY ADOPT REGULATIONS TO CARRY OUT THIS
11 SECTION.
12 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
13 October 1, 2025.
