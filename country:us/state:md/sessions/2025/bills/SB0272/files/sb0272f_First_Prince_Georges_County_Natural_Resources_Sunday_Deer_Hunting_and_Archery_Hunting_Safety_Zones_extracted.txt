2 Prince George’s County – Natural Resources – Sunday Deer Hunting and
3 Archery Hunting Safety Zones
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing the Department of Natural Resources to allow a person to
5 hunt deer on each Sunday of the deer bow hunting season in Prince George’s County;
6 adding Prince George’s County to the list of counties in which the safety zone for
7 archery hunters extends a certain distance from certain structures; and generally
8 relating to hunting in Prince George’s County.
