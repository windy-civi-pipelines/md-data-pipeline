CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Registered Apprenticeship Investments for a Stronger Economy (RAISE) Act
3 FOR the purpose of authorizing the State Board of Plumbing to waive the license
4 examination requirement for a journey plumber license; authorizing the Secretary
5 of Labor to waive the examination for certain licenses; altering the duties of the
6 Maryland Apprenticeship and Training Council; repealing the Youth Apprenticeship
7 Advisory Committee; establishing the Maryland Office of Registered Apprenticeship
8 Development within the Maryland Apprenticeship and Training Program;
9 establishing the Registered Apprenticeship Qualified Intermediary Program within
10 the Maryland Apprenticeship and Training Program; establishing the Maryland
11 Apprenticeship Incentive Pay Per Apprentice Program within the Maryland
12 Department of Labor; establishing the Maryland Apprenticeship Incentive Pay Per
13 Apprentice Program Fund as a special, nonlapsing fund; requiring that the interest
14 earnings of the Fund remain in the Fund; establishing the Registered
15 Apprenticeship Development Advisory Board in the Department; applying certain
16 provisions of this Act retroactively; and generally relating to registered
17 apprenticeships.
