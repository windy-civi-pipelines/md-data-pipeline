15 10–105.

17 income] CORPORATION’S MARYLAND TAXABLE INCOME IS:
18 (1) FOR A TAXABLE YEAR BEGINNING AFTER DECEMBER 31, 2024,
19 BUT BEFORE JANUARY 1, 2026, 8.25%;
