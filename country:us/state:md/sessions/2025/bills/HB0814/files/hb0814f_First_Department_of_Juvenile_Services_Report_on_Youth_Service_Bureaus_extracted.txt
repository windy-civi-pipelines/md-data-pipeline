By: Delegate Taylor
Introduced and read first time: January 29, 2025
Assigned to: Judiciary
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Department of Juvenile Services – Report on Youth Service Bureaus
3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Department of Juvenile Services to submit a report
4 regarding youth service bureaus on or before a certain date each year; and generally
5 relating to the Department of Juvenile Services and youth service bureaus.
6 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
7 Article – Human Services
8 Section 9–101(a) and (b)
9 Annotated Code of Maryland
10 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
11 BY adding to
12 Article – Human Services
13 Section 9–224
14 Annotated Code of Maryland
15 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
16 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
17 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
18 Article – Human Services
19 9–101.
20 (a) In this title the following words have the meanings indicated.
21 (b) “Department” means the Department of Juvenile Services.
22 9–224.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb0814*
2 HOUSE BILL 814
1 ON OR BEFORE OCTOBER 1, 2025, AND EACH OCTOBER 1 THEREAFTER, THE
2 DEPARTMENT SHALL REPORT TO THE SENATE JUDICIAL PROCEEDINGS
3 COMMITTEE AND THE HOUSE JUDICIARY COMMITTEE, IN ACCORDANCE WITH §
4 2–1257 OF THE STATE GOVERNMENT ARTICLE, ON:
5 (1) EFFORTS BY THE DEPARTMENT TO PROMOTE PREDELINQUENT
6 PROGRAMS, INCLUDING YOUTH SERVICE BUREAUS;
7 (2) EFFORTS BY THE DEPARTMENT TO COLLABORATE WITH AND
8 PROVIDE TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE TO LOCAL GOVERNMENTS REGARDING THE
9 ESTABLISHMENT, USE, AND FUNDING OF YOUTH SERVICE BUREAUS;
10 (3) AN ASSESSMENT OF THE PROGRAMS AND ACTIVITIES CONDUCTED
11 BY YOUTH SERVICE BUREAUS; AND
12 (4) ANY OTHER EFFORTS TO PREVENT YOUTH OFFENSES.
13 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
14 1, 2025.
