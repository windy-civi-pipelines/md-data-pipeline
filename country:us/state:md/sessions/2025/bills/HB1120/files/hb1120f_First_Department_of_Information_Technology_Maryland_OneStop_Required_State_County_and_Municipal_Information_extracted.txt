Section 1:
P1, L6 5lr1937
CF SB 497
By: Delegates Vogel and Schmidt
Introduced and read first time: February 5, 2025
Assigned to: Health and Government Operations
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Department of Information Technology – Maryland OneStop – Required State,
3 County, and Municipal Information
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Department of Information Technology, on or before a
5 certain date and periodically thereafter, to update certain information available on
6 the Maryland OneStop portal; requiring, beginning on a certain date, each county
7 and municipal corporation, in coordination with the Department, to make available
8 on Maryland OneStop certain information; requiring each county and municipal
9 corporation to periodically update certain information available on Maryland
10 OneStop; and generally relating to State, county, and municipal information
11 available on Maryland OneStop.
12 BY adding to
13 Article – State Finance and Procurement
14 Section 3.5–319
15 Annotated Code of Maryland
16 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
17 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
18 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
19 Article – State Finance and Procurement
20 3.5–319.
21 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “MARYLAND ONESTOP” MEANS THE
22 INTERNET–BASED STATEWIDE LICENSING PORTAL MANAGED BY THE DEPARTMENT
23 OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb1120*
2 HOUSE BILL 1120
1 (B) ON OR BEFORE DECEMBER 1, 2025, AND EVERY 2 MONTHS
2 THEREAFTER, IN COORDINATION WITH EACH UNIT OF STATE GOVERNMENT, THE
3 DEPARTMENT SHALL UPDATE AS NEEDED ALL LICENSES, FORMS, CERTIFICATES,
4 PERMITS, APPLICATIONS, AND REGISTRATIONS AVAILABLE ON MARYLAND
5 ONESTOP.
6 (C) (1) ON OR BEFORE JULY 1, 2026, IN COORDINATION WITH THE
7 DEPARTMENT, EACH COUNTY AND MUNICIPAL CORPORATION SHALL MAKE
8 AVAILABLE ON MARYLAND ONESTOP ALL COUNTY OR MUNICIPAL LICENSES,
9 FORMS, CERTIFICATES, PERMITS, APPLICATIONS, AND REGISTRATIONS.
10 (2) ON OR BEFORE SEPTEMBER 1, 2026, AND EVERY 2 MONTHS
11 THEREAFTER, EACH COUNTY AND MUNICIPAL CORPORATION SHALL UPDATE AS
12 NEEDED ALL COUNTY OR MUNICIPAL LICENSES, FORMS, CERTIFICATES, PERMITS,
13 APPLICATIONS, AND REGISTRATIONS AVAILABLE ON MARYLAND ONESTOP.
14 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
15 October 1, 2025.
