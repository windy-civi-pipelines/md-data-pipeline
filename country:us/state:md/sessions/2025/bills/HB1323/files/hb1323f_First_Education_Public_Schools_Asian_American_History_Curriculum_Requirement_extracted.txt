By: Delegates Valderrama and Wu

Assigned to: Ways and Means
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Education – Public Schools – Asian American History Curriculum Requirement
3 FOR the purpose of requiring the State Board of Education to develop curriculum content
4 standards for a unit of instruction on Asian American history for public schools in
5 the State; requiring each county board of education to implement the Asian
6 American history curriculum content standards in each public school in the county,
7 beginning in a certain school year; requiring each county superintendent of schools
8 to ensure compliance with this Act; and generally relating to an Asian American
9 history curriculum requirement for public schools in the State.
10 BY adding to
11 Article – Education
12 Section 7–205.5
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
15 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
16 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
17 Article – Education
18 7–205.5.
19 (A) (1) THE STATE BOARD SHALL DEVELOP CURRICULUM CONTENT
20 STANDARDS FOR A UNIT OF INSTRUCTION ON ASIAN AMERICAN HISTORY FOR
21 PUBLIC SCHOOLS IN THE STATE.
22 (2) THE ASIAN AMERICAN HISTORY CURRICULUM CONTENT
23 STANDARDS SHALL:
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb1323*
2 HOUSE BILL 1323
1 (I) ENABLE STUDENTS TO RESPECT THE DIGNITY OF ALL
2 RACES AND ALL PEOPLE; AND
3 (II) REQUIRE AGE–APPROPRIATE INSTRUCTION ON THE
4 FOLLOWING TOPICS:
5 1. THE EVENTS OF ASIAN AMERICAN HISTORY;
6 2. ASIAN AMERICAN HISTORY IN MARYLAND AND THE
7 MID–ATLANTIC REGION; AND
8 3. CONTRIBUTIONS MADE BY ASIAN AMERICANS,
9 INCLUDING THEIR:
10 A. ROLES IN THE ADVANCEMENT OF CIVIL RIGHTS FROM
11 THE 19TH CENTURY ONWARD;
12 B. ACCOMPLISHMENTS IN GOVERNMENT, THE ARTS,
13 HUMANITIES, AND SCIENCE; AND
14 C. CONTRIBUTIONS AS A COMMUNITY TO THE
15 ECONOMIC, CULTURAL, SOCIAL, AND POLITICAL DEVELOPMENT OF THE UNITED
16 STATES.
17 (3) THE STATE BOARD MAY PREPARE AND MAKE AVAILABLE
18 INSTRUCTIONAL MATERIALS, INCLUDING THE PUBLIC BROADCASTING SERVICE’S
19 INSTRUCTIONAL MATERIALS ON ASIAN AMERICANS, THAT MAY BE USED AS A GUIDE
20 FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF ASIAN AMERICAN HISTORY CURRICULUM CONTENT
21 STANDARDS.
22 (B) (1) BEGINNING IN THE 2026–2027 SCHOOL YEAR, EACH COUNTY
23 BOARD SHALL IMPLEMENT THE ASIAN AMERICAN HISTORY CURRICULUM CONTENT
24 STANDARDS DEVELOPED BY THE STATE BOARD UNDER SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS
25 SECTION IN EACH PUBLIC SCHOOL IN THE COUNTY.
26 (2) (I) EACH COUNTY BOARD SHALL DETERMINE THE
27 APPROPRIATE AMOUNT OF INSTRUCTIONAL TIME NECESSARY TO SATISFY THE
28 REQUIREMENT FOR A UNIT OF INSTRUCTION ON ASIAN AMERICAN HISTORY.
29 (II) EACH COUNTY BOARD SHALL ENSURE THAT A UNIT OF
30 INSTRUCTION ON ASIAN AMERICAN HISTORY IS:
31 1. TAUGHT AT LEAST ONCE DURING ELEMENTARY
HOUSE BILL 1323 3
1 SCHOOL;
2 2. INCLUDED AT LEAST ONCE IN A REQUIRED HISTORY
3 COURSE IN MIDDLE SCHOOL; AND
4 3. INCLUDED AT LEAST ONCE IN A HISTORY COURSE
5 REQUIRED TO GRADUATE FROM HIGH SCHOOL.
6 (3) A COUNTY BOARD MAY DEVELOP AN ONLINE COURSE TO SATISFY
7 THE REQUIREMENTS OF THIS SECTION.
8 (C) EACH COUNTY SUPERINTENDENT SHALL ENSURE COMPLIANCE WITH
9 THE REQUIREMENTS OF THIS SECTION.
10 (D) THE STATE BOARD MAY ADOPT REGULATIONS TO CARRY OUT THIS
11 SECTION.
12 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
13 1, 2025.
