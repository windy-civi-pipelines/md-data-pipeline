1 AN ACT concerning
2 State Finance and Procurement – State Leasing of Real Property
3 FOR the purpose of increasing the noncompetitive threshold for State leases of real
4 property; altering the time frame and platform for advertising a need of the State to
5 rent building space; and generally relating to the leasing of real property for State
6 use.
7 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
8 Article – State Finance and Procurement
9 Section 4–318(5) and 4–320
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
12 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
13 Article – State Finance and Procurement
14 Section 13–101(a) and (c)
15 Annotated Code of Maryland
16 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
17 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
18 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

6 (a) IN THIS SECTION, “EMARYLAND MARKETPLACE” HAS THE MEANING
7 STATED IN § 13–101 OF THIS ARTICLE.
8 (B) The Department shall advertise any need of the State to rent building space
9 at least [30] 20 days before proposals for a lease are due.
10 [(b)] (C) The Department shall advertise the proposed leasing:
11 (1) [in daily or weekly newspapers with circulation sufficient to provide
12 notice:
13 (1) to the people of the State, OR AT LEAST TO THE PEOPLE OF THE

15 (2) at least to the people of the affected locality] TO THE PEOPLE OF THE
16 STATE, OR AT LEAST TO THE PEOPLE OF THE AFFECTED LOCALITY, AND POTENTIAL
17 BIDDERS BY POSTING A PUBLICATION ON THE DEPARTMENT’S WEBSITE AND
18 EMARYLAND MARKETPLACE.
19 [(c)] (D) The advertisement shall contain:
20 (1) the number of square feet proposed to be leased; and
21 (2) the general area in which the State desires to lease the space.
22 13–101.
23 (a) In this subtitle the following words have the meanings indicated.
24 (c) “eMaryland Marketplace” or “eMaryland Marketplace Advantage” means the
25 Internet–based procurement system managed by the Department of General Services.
26 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
27 October 1, 2025.
