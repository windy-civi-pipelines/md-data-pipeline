A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Health Occupations Boards – Notation of Veteran Status and Eligibility for
3 Benefits
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing health occupations boards to establish a certain process to
5 include a notation indicating veteran status on a license, certificate, or registration
6 and assist in determining eligibility for State and federal veterans benefits; and
7 generally relating to veterans and health occupations boards.
8 BY adding to
9 Article – Health Occupations
10 Section 1–707
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
15 Article – Health Occupations
16 1–707.
17 (A) EACH HEALTH OCCUPATIONS BOARD MAY ESTABLISH A PROCESS TO
18 INCLUDE A NOTATION INDICATING VETERAN STATUS ON A LICENSE, CERTIFICATE,
19 OR REGISTRATION AND TO ASSIST IN DETERMINING ELIGIBILITY FOR STATE AND
20 FEDERAL VETERANS BENEFITS IN ACCORDANCE WITH THIS SECTION.
21 (B) IF A HEALTH OCCUPATIONS BOARD ESTABLISHES THE PROCESS
22 DESCRIBED IN SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION, AN APPLICATION FOR A LICENSE,
23 CERTIFICATE, OR REGISTRATION SHALL ALLOW AN APPLICANT TO:
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *sb0067*
2 SENATE BILL 67
1 (1) PRESENT A CERTIFICATION OF VETERAN STATUS OBTAINED
2 FROM THE DEPARTMENT OF VETERANS AND MILITARY FAMILIES IN ACCORDANCE
3 WITH § 9–905 OF THE STATE GOVERNMENT ARTICLE, A DD FORM 214, OR OTHER
4 DOCUMENTATION ACCEPTABLE TO THE HEALTH OCCUPATIONS BOARD CERTIFYING
5 VETERAN STATUS; AND
6 (2) INDICATE THAT THE APPLICANT CONSENTS TO:
7 (I) HAVING A NOTATION ON THE APPLICANT’S LICENSE,
8 CERTIFICATE, OR REGISTRATION INDICATING THAT THE APPLICANT IS A VETERAN;
9 AND
10 (II) BEING CONTACTED BY APPROPRIATE EXECUTIVE BRANCH
11 AGENCIES REGARDING THE APPLICANT’S ELIGIBILITY FOR STATE OR FEDERAL
12 VETERANS BENEFITS.
13 (C) IF AN APPLICANT PRESENTS THE REQUIRED DOCUMENTATION AND
14 CONSENTS IN ACCORDANCE WITH SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION, A HEALTH
15 OCCUPATIONS BOARD SHALL:
16 (1) INCLUDE ON THE APPLICANT’S LICENSE, CERTIFICATION, OR
17 REGISTRATION A NOTATION INDICATING THAT THE APPLICANT IS A VETERAN; AND
18 (2) IN ACCORDANCE WITH §§ 4–304 THROUGH 4–325 OF THE
19 GENERAL PROVISIONS ARTICLE AND ANY OTHER APPLICABLE LAW, AND ON
20 REQUEST FROM AN EXECUTIVE BRANCH AGENCY, ELECTRONICALLY TRANSMIT TO
21 THE EXECUTIVE BRANCH AGENCY APPROPRIATE INFORMATION ABOUT THE
22 APPLICANT RELEVANT TO DETERMINING THE APPLICANT’S ELIGIBILITY FOR STATE
23 OR FEDERAL VETERANS BENEFITS.
24 (D) EACH HEALTH OCCUPATIONS BOARD MAY ADOPT REGULATIONS TO
25 CARRY OUT THE PROVISIONS OF THIS SECTION.
26 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
27 October 1, 2025.
