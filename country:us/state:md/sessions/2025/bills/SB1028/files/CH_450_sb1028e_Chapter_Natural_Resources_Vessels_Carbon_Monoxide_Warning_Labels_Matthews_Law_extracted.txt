Section 1:
WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 450
Chapter 450
AN ACT concerning
Natural Resources – Vessels – Carbon Monoxide Warning Labels
(Matthew’s Law)
FOR the purpose of prohibiting a person from operating a gasoline–powered motorized
gasoline–powered vessel manufactured after a certain date that has a cabin or other
closed living space accommodation spaces, swim platforms, or aft lounging areas on
the waters of the State unless the vessel has carbon monoxide warning labels affixed
in conspicuous locations in certain areas of the vessel; prohibiting the Department
of Natural Resources from imposing a certain penalty; and generally relating to the
State Boat Act.
BY adding to
Article – Natural Resources

Section 2:
Section 8–740.2
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2023 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)

Section 3:
SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
Article – Natural Resources
8–740.2.
(A) IN THIS SECTION, “MOTORIZED VESSEL” DOES NOT INCLUDE A VESSEL
POWERED BY AN ELECTRIC MOTOR.
(B) (A) A PERSON MAY NOT OPERATE A GASOLINE–POWERED MOTORIZED
GASOLINE–POWERED VESSEL MANUFACTURED AFTER JULY 31, 2007, THAT HAS A
CABIN OR OTHER CLOSED LIVING SPACE ACCOMMODATION SPACES, SWIM
PLATFORMS, OR AFT LOUNGING AREAS ON THE WATERS OF THE STATE UNLESS THE
VESSEL HAS CARBON MONOXIDE WARNING LABELS AFFIXED IN CONSPICUOUS
LOCATIONS IN THE VICINITY OF BOTH THE TRANSOM AND THE HELM OF THE VESSEL.
(C) THE (B) THE DEPARTMENT MAY NOT IMPOSE A PENALTY ON A
PERSON THAT VIOLATES THE PROVISIONS OF THIS SECTION.

Section 4:
SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
October 1, 2025.
– 1 –
Ch. 450 2025 LAWS OF MARYLAND
Approved by the Governor, May 13, 2025.
