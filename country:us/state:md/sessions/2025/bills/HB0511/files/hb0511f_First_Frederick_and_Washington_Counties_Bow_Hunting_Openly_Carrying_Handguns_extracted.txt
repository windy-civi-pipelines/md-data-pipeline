HB 1480/24 – ENT CF SB 449
By: Frederick County Delegation
Introduced and read first time: January 22, 2025
Assigned to: Environment and Transportation
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Frederick and Washington Counties – Bow Hunting – Openly Carrying
3 Handguns
4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting the Department of Natural Resources from prohibiting
5 certain licensed bow hunters from openly carrying a handgun under certain
6 circumstances in Frederick County and Washington County during deer hunting
7 season; and generally relating to bow hunting in Frederick County and Washington
8 County.
9 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
10 Article – Natural Resources
11 Section 10–408
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
14 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
16 Article – Natural Resources
17 10–408.
18 (a) In this section, “handgun” means a firearm:
19 (1) With a barrel length not exceeding 6 inches; and
20 (2) That does not have a scope or an electronic device attached.
21 (b) (1) Except as provided in paragraph (2) of this subsection, the Department
22 shall:
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb0511*
2 HOUSE BILL 511
1 (i) Prescribe by regulation the means or weapons for hunting
2 designated wildlife; and
3 (ii) Set forth any restrictions relating to weapons used to hunt
4 designated wildlife, including the amount and size of ammunition for designated game
5 birds or mammals.
6 (2) The Department may not prohibit a licensed bow hunter from openly
7 carrying a handgun that the hunter is otherwise authorized to carry under § 4–203 of the
8 Criminal Law Article if the bow hunter:
9 (i) Is at least 21 years old;
10 (ii) Is hunting in [deer]:
11 1. DEER management Region A as defined by the
12 Department’s Guide to Hunting and Trapping; OR
13 2. FREDERICK COUNTY OR WASHINGTON COUNTY
14 DURING DEER HUNTING SEASON;
15 (iii) Is carrying the handgun for personal protection; and
16 (iv) Does not use the handgun to kill wildlife wounded by a vertical
17 bow or crossbow.
18 (c) The Department shall make the regulations available for distribution with
19 each hunting license purchased.
20 (d) This section does not authorize the Department to restrict the use of firearms
21 except in the activity of hunting designated wildlife.
22 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
23 1, 2025.
