1 AN ACT concerning
2 Financial Institutions – Consumer Credit – Application of Licensing
3 Requirements
4 (Maryland Secondary Market Stability Act of 2025)
5 FOR the purpose of providing that certain provisions of law governing the licensing of
6 providers of certain financial services in the State do not apply to a certain person
7 that acquires or is assigned a certain mortgage, mortgage loan, or installment loan
8 under certain circumstances or a certain trust that acquires or is assigned a certain
9 mortgage loan under certain circumstances; establishing the Maryland Licensing
10 Workgroup to study and make recommendations on licensing requirements for
11 persons that provide financial services in the State; and generally relating to
12 consumer credit lending licensing requirements and the Maryland Licensing
13 Workgroup.
14 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
