J1 5lr2464
CF 5lr3143
By: Delegate Bagnall
Introduced and read first time: January 27, 2025
Assigned to: Health and Government Operations
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public Health – Nonopioid Advance Directives
3 FOR the purpose of authorizing a competent individual to make a nonopioid advance
4 directive stating that the individual refuses the offer or administration of opioid
5 medication including during an emergency; requiring the Maryland Department of
6 Health to develop a model nonopioid advance directive form and make the form
7 available on the Department’s website; and generally relating to nonopioid advance
8 directives.
9 BY adding to
10 Article – Health – General
11 Section 5–602.2
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
14 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
16 Article – Health – General
17 5–602.2.
18 (A) A COMPETENT INDIVIDUAL MAY MAKE A NONOPIOID ADVANCE
19 DIRECTIVE STATING THAT THE INDIVIDUAL REFUSES THE OFFER OR
20 ADMINISTRATION OF ANY OPIOID MEDICATION INCLUDING DURING AN EMERGENCY
21 WHERE THE INDIVIDUAL IS UNABLE TO COMMUNICATE THE INDIVIDUAL’S
22 PREFERENCE REGARDING THE USE OF OPIOIDS.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb0737*
2 HOUSE BILL 737
1 (B) (1) AN INDIVIDUAL MAKING A NONOPIOID ADVANCE DIRECTIVE
2 SHALL FOLLOW THE PROCEDURES FOR MAKING AN ADVANCE DIRECTIVE PROVIDED
3 UNDER § 5–602 OF THIS SUBTITLE.
4 (2) THE PROCEDURES PROVIDED UNDER § 5–604 OF THIS SUBTITLE
5 FOR THE REVOCATION OF AN ADVANCE DIRECTIVE SHALL APPLY TO THE
6 REVOCATION OF A NONOPIOID ADVANCE DIRECTIVE.
7 (C) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL DEVELOP A MODEL NONOPIOID ADVANCE
8 DIRECTIVE FORM AND MAKE THE FORM AVAILABLE ON THE DEPARTMENT’S
9 WEBSITE.
10 (D) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL ADOPT REGULATIONS TO CARRY OUT THIS
11 SECTION.
12 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
13 October 1, 2025.
