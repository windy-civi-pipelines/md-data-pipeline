P1 5lr2138
By: Delegates R. Lewis, Allen, Embry, Healey, Holmes, Lehman, Shetty, Smith,
Stein, Stewart, Woods, and Young
Introduced and read first time: January 29, 2025
Assigned to: Health and Government Operations
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 State Designations – State Carnivorous Plant – Purple Pitcherplant
3 FOR the purpose of designating the Purple Pitcherplant as the State carnivorous plant;
4 and generally relating to the State carnivorous plant.
5 BY renumbering
6 Article – General Provisions
7 Section 7–302 through 7–310
8 to be Section 7–303 through 7–311, respectively
9 Annotated Code of Maryland
10 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
11 BY adding to
12 Article – General Provisions
13 Section 7–302
14 Annotated Code of Maryland
15 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
16 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
17 That Section(s) 7–302 through 7–310 of Article – General Provisions of the Annotated Code
18 of Maryland be renumbered to be Section(s) 7–303 through 7–311, respectively.
19 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That the Laws of Maryland read
20 as follows:
21 Article – General Provisions
22 7–302.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
