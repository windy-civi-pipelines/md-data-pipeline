A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Anne Arundel County – Board of License Commissioners – Chief Inspector –
3 Compensation
4 FOR the purpose of increasing the minimum and maximum salary amounts for the chief
5 inspector employed by the Board of License Commissioners for Anne Arundel
6 County; and generally relating to alcoholic beverages and the Board of License
7 Commissioners for Anne Arundel County.
8 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
9 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
10 Section 11–102
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2024 Replacement Volume)
13 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
14 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
15 Section 11–204(b)(2)
16 Annotated Code of Maryland
17 (2024 Replacement Volume)
18 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
19 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
20 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
21 11–102.
22 This title applies only in Anne Arundel County.
23 11–204.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *sb0570*
2 SENATE BILL 570
1 (b) (2) The Board shall employ:
2 (i) a full–time executive director whose annual salary shall be fixed
3 by the Board as in a general county classified salary schedule, within pay grade 17;
4 (ii) a full–time administrator whose annual salary shall be fixed by
5 the Board as in a general county classified salary schedule, within pay grade 16;
6 (iii) two full–time secretaries whose annual salaries shall be fixed by
7 the Board as in a general county classified salary schedule, within pay grade 13;
8 (iv) a full–time chief inspector whose annual salary shall be fixed by
9 the Board as in a general county classified salary schedule, within non–represented pay
10 schedule [NR–05] NR–15; and
11 (v) an attorney at an annual salary of $60,000.
12 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
13 1, 2025.
