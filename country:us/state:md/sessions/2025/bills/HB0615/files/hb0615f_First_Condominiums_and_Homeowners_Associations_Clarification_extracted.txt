N1 5lr2870
By: Delegate Holmes
Introduced and read first time: January 23, 2025
Assigned to: Environment and Transportation
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Condominiums and Homeowners Associations – Clarification
3 FOR the purpose of clarifying terminology in provisions relating to the proposed budget for
4 a condominium and the appointment of a receiver for a homeowners association; and
5 generally relating to condominiums and homeowners associations.
6 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
7 Article – Real Property
8 Section 11–109.2(d) and 11B–111.5(a)
9 Annotated Code of Maryland
10 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
11 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
12 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
13 Article – Real Property
14 11–109.2.
15 (d) (1) The budget shall be adopted at an open meeting of the council of unit
16 owners or any other body to which the council of unit owners delegates responsibilities for
17 preparing and adopting the budget.
18 (2) (i) The council of unit owners or other governing body of unit owners
19 shall submit the adopted annual budget to the unit owners not more than 30 days after the
20 meeting at which the budget was adopted.
21 (ii) The adopted annual budget may be submitted to each unit owner
22 by electronic transmission, by posting on the condominium association’s home page, or by
23 inclusion in the [homeowners] CONDOMINIUM association’s newsletter.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb0615*
2 HOUSE BILL 615
1 11B–111.5.
2 (a) If a homeowners association fails to fill vacancies on the governing body
3 sufficient to constitute a quorum in accordance with the bylaws, three or more owners of
4 lots may petition the circuit court for the county where the [condominium] HOMEOWNERS
5 ASSOCIATION is located to appoint a receiver to manage the affairs of the homeowners
6 association.
7 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
8 October 1, 2025.
