CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Sickle Cell Disease – Institutions of Higher Education – Policies, Procedures,
3 and Educational Campaigns
4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting an institution of higher education from denying access or
5 services to a student based on the student’s diagnosis of sickle cell disease; requiring
6 an institution of higher education to provide reasonable accommodations for
7 students with sickle cell disease; requiring the Maryland Department of Health to
8 develop an education and awareness campaign for institutions of higher education
9 in the State regarding students with sickle cell disease; requiring the Department,
10 in consultation with the Maryland Higher Education Commission, to develop
11 uniform procedures for institutions of higher education in the State to follow in
12 assessing, supporting, and treating students with sickle cell disease; requiring the
13 Department to develop or compile materials to provide a comprehensive education
14 on sickle cell disease to certain faculty and staff at institutions of higher education
15 in the State; and generally relating to sickle cell disease and institutions of higher
16 education.
17 BY adding to
18 Article – Education
19 Section 15–140
20 Annotated Code of Maryland
21 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)

1 BY adding to

3 Section 18–510 through 18–512
4 Annotated Code of Maryland

6 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
7 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
8 Article – Education
9 15–140.
10 (A) AN INSTITUTION OF HIGHER EDUCATION:
11 (1) SHALL PROVIDE REASONABLE ACCOMMODATIONS TO A STUDENT
12 WITH SICKLE CELL DISEASE; AND
13 (2) MAY NOT DENY ACCESS TO FACILITIES OR SERVICES TO A
14 STUDENT BASED ON THE STUDENT’S DIAGNOSIS OF SICKLE CELL DISEASE.
15 (B) AN INSTITUTION OF HIGHER EDUCATION SHALL ESTABLISH A
16 PROCEDURE THROUGH WHICH A PERSON MAY REPORT AN ALLEGED VIOLATION OF
17 SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION.
18 (C) AN INSTITUTION OF HIGHER EDUCATION SHALL PUBLICIZE IN A
19 MANNER THAT IS CLEAR AND ACCESSIBLE TO STUDENTS:
20 (1) THE REQUIREMENTS OF SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION; AND
21 (2) THE REPORTING PROCEDURE ESTABLISHED UNDER SUBSECTION
22 (B) OF THIS SECTION.
23 Article – Health – General
24 18–510.
25 (A) THE DEPARTMENT, IN CONSULTATION WITH SICKLE CELL DISEASE
26 TREATMENT PROVIDERS AND COMMUNITY–BASED ORGANIZATIONS, SHALL
27 DEVELOP AN EDUCATION AND AWARENESS CAMPAIGN FOR INSTITUTIONS OF
28 HIGHER EDUCATION IN THE STATE REGARDING STUDENTS WITH SICKLE CELL
29 DISEASE.
HOUSE BILL 1352 3
1 (B) THE PROGRAM DEVELOPED UNDER SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION
2 SHALL:
3 (1) DESCRIBE CHALLENGES FACED BY YOUNG ADULTS LIVING WITH
4 SICKLE CELL DISEASE, WITH AN EMPHASIS ON CHALLENGES FACED DURING HEALTH
5 CRISES RELATED TO SICKLE CELL DISEASE; AND
6 (2) PROVIDE INFORMATION ON ACCOMMODATIONS AND SUPPORT
7 THAT INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER EDUCATION CAN PROVIDE TO STUDENTS WITH
8 SICKLE CELL DISEASE TO ENABLE THE STUDENTS TO SUCCEED ACADEMICALLY
9 WHILE MANAGING SICKLE CELL DISEASE.
10 18–511.
11 (A) THE DEPARTMENT, IN CONSULTATION WITH THE MARYLAND HIGHER
12 EDUCATION COMMISSION, SHALL DEVELOP UNIFORM PROCEDURES FOR
13 INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER EDUCATION IN THE STATE TO FOLLOW IN ASSESSING,
14 SUPPORTING, AND TREATING STUDENTS WITH SICKLE CELL DISEASE.
15 (B) THE DEPARTMENT AND THE MARYLAND HIGHER EDUCATION
16 COMMISSION JOINTLY SHALL:
17 (1) BEGINNING DECEMBER 1, 2025, PROVIDE TECHNICAL
18 ASSISTANCE TO INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER EDUCATION TO:
19 (I) IMPLEMENT THE PROCEDURES DEVELOPED UNDER THIS
20 SECTION; AND
21 (II) INSTRUCT PERSONNEL OF INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER
22 EDUCATION REGARDING THE PROCEDURES DEVELOPED UNDER THIS SECTION; AND
23 (2) ON OR BEFORE DECEMBER 1, 2025, DEVELOP A PROCESS TO
24 MONITOR THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THE PROCEDURES BY INSTITUTIONS OF
25 HIGHER EDUCATION.
26 18–512.
27 (A) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL DEVELOP OR COMPILE MATERIALS TO
28 PROVIDE HEALTH PERSONNEL, FACULTY, AND ADMINISTRATIVE STAFF EMPLOYED
29 AT AN INSTITUTION OF HIGHER EDUCATION IN THE STATE WITH A COMPREHENSIVE
30 EDUCATION ON SICKLE CELL DISEASE.
4 HOUSE BILL 1352
1 (B) THE MATERIALS DEVELOPED OR COMPILED UNDER THIS SECTION
2 SHALL FOCUS ON ENHANCING UNDERSTANDING OF SICKLE CELL DISEASE,
3 REDUCING STIGMAS, AND FOSTERING A SUPPORTIVE LEARNING ENVIRONMENT FOR
4 STUDENTS WITH SICKLE CELL DISEASE.
5 (C) EACH INSTITUTE OF HIGHER EDUCATION IS ENCOURAGED TO CONDUCT
6 AWARENESS CAMPAIGNS AND EDUCATIONAL SESSIONS BASED ON THE MATERIALS
7 DEVELOPED OR COMPILED UNDER THIS SECTION AS FINANCIAL RESOURCES ALLOW.
8 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
9 October 1, 2025.
Approved:
