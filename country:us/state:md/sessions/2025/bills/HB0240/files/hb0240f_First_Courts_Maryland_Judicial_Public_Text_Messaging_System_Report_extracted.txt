D1 5lr1660
HB 978/24 – JUD (PRE–FILED)
By: Delegate Young
Requested: November 1, 2024
Introduced and read first time: January 8, 2025
Assigned to: Judiciary
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Courts – Maryland Judicial Public Text Messaging System – Report
3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Administrative Office of the Courts to provide an annual
4 report to the General Assembly containing certain information on the Maryland
5 Judicial Public Text Messaging System; and generally relating to the Maryland
6 Judicial Public Text Messaging System.
7 BY adding to
8 Article – Courts and Judicial Proceedings
9 Section 13–103
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
14 Article – Courts and Judicial Proceedings
15 13–103.
16 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
17 INDICATED.
18 (2) “MARYLAND JUDICIAL PUBLIC TEXT MESSAGING SYSTEM”
19 MEANS TEXT NOTIFICATION ABOUT COURT EVENTS AND RELATED CASE
20 INFORMATION PERMANENTLY AUTHORIZED UNDER THE ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER
21 OF THE CHIEF JUSTICE OF THE SUPREME COURT OF MARYLAND ON APRIL 22,
22 2020.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb0240*
2 HOUSE BILL 240
1 (3) “SYSTEM” MEANS THE MARYLAND JUDICIAL PUBLIC TEXT
2 MESSAGING SYSTEM.
3 (B) BEGINNING DECEMBER 1, 2025, AND EACH DECEMBER 1 THEREAFTER,
4 THE ADMINISTRATIVE OFFICE OF THE COURTS SHALL REPORT TO THE GENERAL
5 ASSEMBLY, IN ACCORDANCE WITH § 2–1257 OF THE STATE GOVERNMENT ARTICLE,
6 ON THE MARYLAND JUDICIAL PUBLIC TEXT MESSAGING SYSTEM.
7 (C) THE REPORT SHALL INCLUDE THE FOLLOWING INFORMATION ON THE
8 SYSTEM FOR THE PRIOR FISCAL YEAR:
9 (1) THE NUMBER OF INDIVIDUALS WHO REQUESTED NOTIFICATION
10 UNDER THE SYSTEM;
11 (2) THE NUMBER OF INDIVIDUALS WHO DECLINED TO RECEIVE
12 NOTIFICATION;
13 (3) THE TOTAL NUMBER OF NOTIFICATIONS SENT REGARDING
14 PROCEEDINGS;
15 (4) THE NUMBER OF NOTIFICATIONS SENT AT LEAST 7 DAYS BEFORE
16 A PROCEEDING;
17 (5) THE NUMBER OF NOTIFICATIONS SENT 6 OR FEWER DAYS BEFORE
18 A PROCEEDING AND THE NUMBER OF THOSE NOTIFICATIONS SENT AT LEAST 1 DAY
19 BEFORE A PROCEEDING;
20 (6) THE NUMBER OF NOTIFICATIONS THAT FAILED TO DELIVER;
21 (7) THE NUMBER OF REQUESTS BY PARTICIPANTS FOR ASSISTANCE;
22 AND
23 (8) THE RATE OF FAILURE TO APPEAR BY PARTICIPANTS IN THE
24 SYSTEM COMPARED WITH THAT OF INDIVIDUALS DECLINING PARTICIPATION IN THE
25 SYSTEM.
26 (D) IN ADDITION TO INFORMATION REQUIRED UNDER SUBSECTION (C) OF
27 THIS SECTION, THE REPORT SHALL INCLUDE A REVIEW OF ANY FUTURE PLANS FOR
28 DEVELOPMENT, EXPANSION, AND IMPLEMENTATION OF THE SYSTEM.
29 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
30 1, 2025.
