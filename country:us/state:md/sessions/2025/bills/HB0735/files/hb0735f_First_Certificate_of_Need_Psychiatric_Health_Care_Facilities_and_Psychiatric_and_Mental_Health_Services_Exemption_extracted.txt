A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Certificate of Need – Psychiatric Health Care Facilities and Psychiatric and
3 Mental Health Services – Exemption
4 FOR the purpose of altering the definition of “medical service” for the purpose of providing
5 an exemption to the certificate of need requirement by removing psychiatry and any
6 subcategory of psychiatry; providing that a certificate of need is not required to
7 establish or operate a psychiatric health care facility or to offer psychiatric or other
8 mental health services at a health care facility; and generally relating to psychiatric
9 health care facilities and psychiatric and mental health services and exemptions to
10 the certificate of need requirement.
11 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
12 Article – Health – General
13 Section 19–120(a) and (j)
14 Annotated Code of Maryland
15 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
16 BY adding to
17 Article – Health – General
18 Section 19–122.1
19 Annotated Code of Maryland
20 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
21 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
22 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
23 Article – Health – General

9 service.

11 (4) “Hospital capital threshold” means the lesser of:
12 (i) 25% of the hospital’s gross regulated charges for the immediately
13 preceding year; or

15 (5) “Limited service hospital” means a health care facility that:
16 (i) Is licensed as a hospital on or after January 1, 1999;
17 (ii) Changes the type or scope of health care services offered by
18 eliminating the facility’s capability to admit or retain patients for overnight hospitalization;
19 (iii) Retains an emergency or urgent care center; and
20 (iv) Complies with the regulations adopted by the Secretary under §
21 19–307.1 of this title.
22 (6) “Medical service” means:
23 (i) Any of the following categories of health care services:
24 1. Medicine, surgery, gynecology, addictions;
25 2. Obstetrics;

18 adjustment for inflation, based on an appropriate index specified by the Commission.
