1 AN ACT concerning
2 Senate Education, Energy, and the Environment Committee and House
3 Environment and Transportation Committee – Code References – Revisions
4 FOR the purpose of altering certain provisions of law to reflect the renaming of the Senate
5 Education, Health, and Environmental Affairs Committee to be the Senate
6 Education, Energy, and the Environment Committee; altering certain provisions of
7 law to reflect the renaming of the House Environmental Matters Committee to be
8 the House Environment and Transportation Committee; altering certain provisions
9 of law to reflect changes to the subject matter jurisdiction of the Senate Education,
10 Energy, and the Environment Committee; altering the membership of the Oversight
11 Committee on Quality of Care in Nursing Homes and Assisted Living Facilities;
12 requiring the publisher of the Annotated Code of Maryland, in consultation with and
13 subject to the approval of the Department of Legislative Services, to correct any
14 cross–references or terminology rendered incorrect by this Act and to describe any
15 corrections made in an editor’s note following the section affected; and generally
16 relating to the Senate Education, Energy, and the Environment Committee and the
17 House Environment and Transportation Committee.
