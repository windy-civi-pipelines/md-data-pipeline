A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Senate Education, Energy, and the Environment Committee and House
3 Environment and Transportation Committee – Code References – Revisions
4 FOR the purpose of altering certain provisions of law to reflect the renaming of the Senate
5 Education, Health, and Environmental Affairs Committee to be the Senate
6 Education, Energy, and the Environment Committee; altering certain provisions of
7 law to reflect the renaming of the House Environmental Matters Committee to be
8 the House Environment and Transportation Committee; altering certain provisions
9 of law to reflect changes to the subject matter jurisdiction of the Senate Education,
10 Energy, and the Environment Committee; requiring the publisher of the Annotated
11 Code of Maryland, in consultation with and subject to the approval of the
12 Department of Legislative Services, to correct any cross–references or terminology
13 rendered incorrect by this Act and to describe any corrections made in an editor’s
14 note following the section affected; and generally relating to the Senate Education,
15 Energy, and the Environment Committee and the House Environment and
16 Transportation Committee.
