By: Delegate Rosenberg

Assigned to: Economic Matters
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Baltimore City – Cigarettes and Other Tobacco Products – Public Notice of
3 Application for County License
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the clerk of the Circuit Court for Baltimore City to publish
5 certain public notices of an application for certain county licenses to sell cigarettes
6 or other tobacco products at retail before issuing a certain license; and generally
7 relating to county retailer licenses and cigarettes and other tobacco products.
8 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
9 Article – Business Regulation
10 Section 16–201(a), (d), and (k), 16–204(b)(1), 16–301, and 16.5–101(a), (b), and (f)
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2024 Replacement Volume)
13 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
14 Article – Business Regulation
15 Section 16–302(a) and 16.5–203(b)
16 Annotated Code of Maryland
17 (2024 Replacement Volume)
18 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
19 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
20 Article – Business Regulation
21 16–201.
22 (a) In this subtitle the following words have the meanings indicated.
23 (d) “Licensed retailer” means a person licensed by the clerk under § 16–205(b) of
24 this subtitle to act as a retailer.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.

1 (k) “Retailer” means a person who:
2 (1) sells cigarettes to consumers through vending machines on fewer than
3 40 premises;
4 (2) otherwise sells cigarettes to consumers; or
5 (3) holds cigarettes for sale to consumers.
6 16–204.
7 (b) (1) An applicant for a license to act as a retailer shall:
8 (i) obtain the county license required under § 16–301 of this title;
9 (ii) submit to the clerk an application for each permanent or
10 temporary place of business located in the same enclosure and operated by the same
11 applicant; and
12 (iii) pay to the clerk a fee of $30.
13 16–301.
14 Whenever a person sells cigarettes at retail in a county, the person must have a
15 county license for:
16 (1) each place of business; and
17 (2) each vending machine, if the applicant sells cigarettes through a
18 vending machine.

25 COUNTY LICENSE UNDER § 16–303 OF THIS SUBTITLE, THE CLERK SHALL PROVIDE
26 PUBLIC NOTICE BY:
HOUSE BILL 1300 3
1 (I) PUBLICATION OF A NOTICE OF THE APPLICATION TWO
2 TIMES FOR 2 SUCCESSIVE WEEKS IN TWO NEWSPAPERS OF GENERAL CIRCULATION

5 2. THAT THE APPLICANT IS APPLYING FOR A COUNTY

7 3. THE LOCATION OF THE BUSINESS DESCRIBED IN THE
8 APPLICATION; AND

10 LOCATION OF THE BUSINESS DESCRIBED IN THE APPLICATION FOR AT LEAST 10
11 DAYS.
12 16.5–101.
13 (a) In this title the following words have the meanings indicated.
14 (b) “County license” means a license issued by the clerk to sell other tobacco
15 products at retail in a county.
16 (f) “Licensed other tobacco products retailer” means a person licensed by the
17 clerk under § 16.5–204(b) of this title to act as an other tobacco products retailer.

24 applicant; and
25 (ii) except as provided in paragraph [(2)] (3) of this subsection, shall
26 pay to the clerk a fee of $15.

28 COUNTY LICENSE UNDER THIS SUBTITLE, THE CLERK SHALL PROVIDE PUBLIC
29 NOTICE BY:

4 1. THE NAME OF THE APPLICANT;
5 2. THAT THE APPLICANT IS APPLYING FOR A COUNTY
6 LICENSE TO SELL OTHER TOBACCO PRODUCTS AT RETAIL IN THE CITY; AND
7 3. THE LOCATION OF THE BUSINESS DESCRIBED IN THE
8 APPLICATION; AND
9 (II) POSTING A NOTICE IN A CONSPICUOUS PLACE AT THE
10 LOCATION OF THE BUSINESS DESCRIBED IN THE APPLICATION FOR AT LEAST 10
11 DAYS.
12 [(2)] (3) A person who has a license issued under Title 16 of this article to
13 act as a cigarette retailer or to act as a special cigarette retailer is not required to pay the
14 license fee.
15 [(3)] (4) The application shall:
16 (i) be made on the form that the clerk requires; and
17 (ii) contain the information that the Executive Director requires.
18 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
19 October 1, 2025.
