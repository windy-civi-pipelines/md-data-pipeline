Section 1:
P4, L6 5lr2696
By: Senator Love
Introduced and read first time: January 28, 2025
Assigned to: Finance
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Employment – Wages – Deductions for Public Employees
3 FOR the purpose of applying to governmental employers certain provisions of the Maryland
4 Wage Payment and Collection Law that prohibit an employer from making
5 deductions from the wage of an employee except for specified purposes; allowing a
6 governmental employer to make a deduction from a public employee’s wage if the
7 deduction is authorized expressly in a compensation or benefit plan adopted by the
8 employer; and generally relating to wage deductions under the Maryland Wage
9 Payment and Collection Law.
10 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
11 Article – Labor and Employment
12 Section 3–503
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2016 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
15 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
16 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
17 Article – Labor and Employment
18 3–503.
19 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “EMPLOYER” INCLUDES A GOVERNMENTAL UNIT.
20 (B) An employer may not make a deduction from the wage of an employee unless
21 the deduction is:
22 (1) ordered by a court of competent jurisdiction;

1 (2) authorized expressly in writing by the employee;
2 (3) allowed by the Commissioner because the employee has received full
3 consideration for the deduction; [or]
4 (4) AUTHORIZED EXPRESSLY IN A COMPENSATION OR BENEFIT PLAN
5 FOR PUBLIC EMPLOYEES, SUCH AS A SUPPLEMENTAL RETIREMENT PLAN, ADOPTED
6 BY A GOVERNMENTAL UNIT; OR
7 [(4)] (5) otherwise made in accordance with any law or any rule or
8 regulation issued by a governmental unit.
9 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
10 October 1, 2025.
