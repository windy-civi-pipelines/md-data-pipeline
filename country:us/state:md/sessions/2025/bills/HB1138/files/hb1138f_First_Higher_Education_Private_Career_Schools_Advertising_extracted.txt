Assigned to: Appropriations
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Higher Education – Private Career Schools – Advertising
3 FOR the purpose of authorizing certain private career schools to advertise certain
4 information and collect certain contact information before receiving a certificate of
5 approval from the Maryland Higher Education Commission to operate a private
6 career school in the State; and generally relating to private career schools.
7 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
8 Article – Education
9 Section 10–101(a) and (j)
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
12 BY adding to
13 Article – Education
14 Section 11–212
15 Annotated Code of Maryland
16 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
17 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
18 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
19 Article – Education
20 10–101.
21 (a) In this division the following words have the meanings indicated.
22 (j) “Private career school” means a privately owned and privately operated
23 institution of postsecondary education other than an institution of higher education that
24 furnishes or offers to furnish programs, whether or not requiring a payment of tuition or

2 HOUSE BILL 1138
1 fee, for the purpose of training, retraining, or upgrading individuals for gainful employment
2 as skilled or semiskilled workers or technicians in recognized occupations or in new and
3 emerging occupations.
4 11–212.
5 (A) A PRIVATE CAREER SCHOOL MAY ADVERTISE AND COLLECT CONTACT
6 INFORMATION FOR POTENTIAL NEW STUDENTS BEFORE RECEIVING A CERTIFICATE
7 OF APPROVAL TO OPERATE IN THE STATE IF:
8 (1) THE PRIVATE CAREER SCHOOL HAS:
9 (I) COMPLETED A MARKET SURVEY;
10 (II) SECURED A LEASE OR OWNS A PROPERTY FOR THE SCHOOL
11 BUILDING;
12 (III) COMPLETED ANY NECESSARY CONSTRUCTION,
13 RENOVATIONS, OR MODIFICATIONS TO THE SCHOOL BUILDING; AND
14 (IV) OBTAINED ALL EQUIPMENT AND PERMITS, EXCEPT FOR THE
15 CERTIFICATE OF APPROVAL, NECESSARY FOR OPERATING THE PRIVATE CAREER
16 SCHOOL;
17 (2) THE PRIVATE CAREER SCHOOL IS LIKELY TO BE ISSUED A
18 CERTIFICATE OF APPROVAL FROM THE COMMISSION; AND
19 (3) THE REQUEST TO BEGIN ADVERTISING IS APPROVED BY THE
20 SECRETARY.
21 (B) ADVERTISING FOR A PRIVATE CAREER SCHOOL APPROVED UNDER
22 SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION:
23 (1) MAY CONTAIN INFORMATION RELATING TO THE OPENING OF THE
24 PRIVATE CAREER SCHOOL, INCLUDING THE ANTICIPATED DATE OF OPENING,
25 PROGRAMS OF INSTRUCTION, AND THE APPLICATION PROCESS; AND
26 (2) SHALL DISCLOSE THAT THE PRIVATE CAREER SCHOOL IS
27 PENDING APPROVAL.
28 (C) THE SECRETARY SHALL ADOPT REGULATIONS SPECIFYING THE
29 PROCESS FOR APPROVAL TO ADVERTISE A PRIVATE CAREER SCHOOL UNDER THIS
30 SECTION.
HOUSE BILL 1138 3
1 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
2 1, 2025.
