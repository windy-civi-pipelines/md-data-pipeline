6 Article – Transportation
7 Section 13–809(b)

9 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)

12 Section 13–809(c)
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
15 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
16 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
17 Article – Transportation
18 13–809.
19 (b) (1) Except as otherwise provided in this part, in addition to any other
20 charge required by the Maryland Vehicle Law, an excise tax is imposed:
21 (i) For each original and each subsequent certificate of title issued
22 in this State for a motor vehicle, a trailer, a semitrailer, a moped, a motor scooter, or an

1 off–highway recreational vehicle for which sales and use tax is not collected at the time of
2 purchase; and
3 (ii) Except as provided in paragraph (2) of this subsection, for each
4 motor vehicle, trailer, or semitrailer that is in interstate operation and registered under §
5 13–109(c) or (d) of this title without a certificate of title.
6 (2) (i) An excise tax of $50 is imposed for the registration of a trailer
7 exempt from the titling requirement under § 13–102(12) of this title.
8 (ii) In a case where the fair market value as defined in subsection
9 (a)(2)(iii)2A of this section applies, the excise tax imposed under this part may not be less
10 than $32.
11 (3) A political subdivision of the State may not impose a sales tax, a use
12 tax, or excise tax on the issuance of a motor vehicle certificate of title.
13 (c) (1) Except as provided in subsection (b)(2) of this section, the tax imposed
14 by this section is [6 percent] 6.75% of the fair market value of the vehicle.
15 (2) If the vehicle formerly was a vehicle exempt from the tax imposed by
16 this section, the tax shall be reduced by any amount previously paid by the present owner
17 as a sales and use tax on the vehicle under Title 11 of the Tax – General Article.
18 (3) (i) If the vehicle was formerly titled and registered in another state
19 and the present owner has paid a sales or excise tax to that state at a rate less than that
20 imposed by this State, then the tax imposed shall apply but at a rate measured by the
21 difference only between the tax rate paid to the other state and the tax rate imposed by this
22 section, if the present owner has not been a Maryland resident for more than 60 days.
23 (ii) If the vehicle was formerly titled and registered in another state
24 and the present owner requests to transfer the vehicle in accordance with § 13–810(c)(1) of
25 this subtitle, the Administration shall change or correct the names contained in the
26 certificate of title:
27 1. At the time the excise tax that is credited or imposed
28 under this section is paid and a new title is issued; and
29 2. Without issuing multiple certificates of title or charging
30 additional fees.
31 (iii) Except as provided in subsection (b)(2) of this section, the
32 minimum tax imposed under this section shall be $100.
33 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
34 1, 2025. This Act shall be applicable to all certificates of title issued on or after July 1, 2025,
35 and to all motor vehicles, trailers, or semitrailers subject to the excise tax that are in
HOUSE BILL 167 3
1 interstate operation and registered under § 13–109(c) or (d) of the Transportation Article
2 without a certificate of title on or after July 1, 2025.
