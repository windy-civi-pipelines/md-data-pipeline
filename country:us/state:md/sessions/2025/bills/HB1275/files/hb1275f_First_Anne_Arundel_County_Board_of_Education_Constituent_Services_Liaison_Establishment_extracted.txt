HB 1395/24 – W&M
By: Delegate Howard
Introduced and read first time: February 7, 2025
Assigned to: Ways and Means
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Anne Arundel County Board of Education – Constituent Services Liaison –
3 Establishment
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Anne Arundel County Board of Education to hire a
5 constituent services liaison before the beginning of a certain school year; requiring
6 the president of the county board, in consultation with counsel for the county board,
7 to negotiate a certain employment agreement; providing that the constituent
8 services liaison is subject to removal by the president of the county board under
9 certain circumstances; requiring the constituent services liaison to provide certain
10 services; providing for the salary of the constituent services liaison; and generally
11 relating to the constituent services liaison of the Anne Arundel County Board of
12 Education.
13 BY adding to
14 Article – Education
15 Section 3–2A–09
16 Annotated Code of Maryland
17 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
18 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
19 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
20 Article – Education
21 3–2A–09.
22 (A) (1) SUBJECT TO PARAGRAPH (2) OF THIS SUBSECTION AND BEFORE
23 THE BEGINNING OF THE 2025–2026 SCHOOL YEAR, THE COUNTY BOARD SHALL HIRE
24 A CONSTITUENT SERVICES LIAISON.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb1275*
2 HOUSE BILL 1275
1 (2) THE PRESIDENT OF THE COUNTY BOARD, IN CONSULTATION WITH
2 COUNSEL FOR THE COUNTY BOARD, SHALL NEGOTIATE THE TERMS OF THE
3 EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT FOR THE CONSTITUENT SERVICES LIAISON.
4 (B) THE CONSTITUENT SERVICES LIAISON SHALL BE SUBJECT TO REMOVAL
5 BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE COUNTY BOARD IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE TERMS OF
6 THE EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT REQUIRED UNDER SUBSECTION (A)(2) OF THIS
7 SECTION.
8 (C) THE CONSTITUENT SERVICES LIAISON SHALL:
9 (1) ACT AS THE LIAISON BETWEEN THE COUNTY BOARD AND
10 MEMBERS OF THE COMMUNITY;
11 (2) ASSIST COMMUNITY MEMBERS IN ACCESSING SERVICES OF ANNE
12 ARUNDEL COUNTY PUBLIC SCHOOLS AND OTHER STATE AND COUNTY
13 EDUCATION–RELATED SERVICES; AND
14 (3) ENGAGE COMMUNITY PARTNERS AND LOCAL ORGANIZATIONS TO
15 ADVANCE THE STRATEGIC GOALS OF ANNE ARUNDEL COUNTY PUBLIC SCHOOLS.
16 (D) THE CONSTITUENT SERVICES LIAISON SHALL RECEIVE AN ANNUAL
17 SALARY OF $120,000.
18 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
19 1, 2025.
