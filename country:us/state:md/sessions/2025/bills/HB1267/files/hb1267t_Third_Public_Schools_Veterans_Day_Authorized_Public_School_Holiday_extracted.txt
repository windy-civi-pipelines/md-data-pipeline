1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public Schools – Veterans’ Day – Authorized Public School Holiday
3 FOR the purpose of authorizing public schools to designate Veterans’ Day as a public school
4 holiday; and generally relating to school holidays for public school students.
5 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
6 Article – Education
7 Section 7–103(c)
8 Annotated Code of Maryland
9 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
10 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
11 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
12 Article – Education
13 7–103.
14 (c) (1) The following days are public school holidays:
15 (i) Thanksgiving Day and the day after;
16 (ii) Christmas Eve and from then through January 1;
17 (iii) Martin Luther King, Jr. Day;
