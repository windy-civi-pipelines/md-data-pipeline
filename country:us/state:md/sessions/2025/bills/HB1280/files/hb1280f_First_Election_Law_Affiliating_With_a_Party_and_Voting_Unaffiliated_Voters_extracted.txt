HB 257/24 – W&M
By: Delegates Qi, Guyton, Ruth, Taveras, and Wu
Introduced and read first time: February 7, 2025
Assigned to: Ways and Means
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Election Law – Affiliating With a Party and Voting – Unaffiliated Voters
3 FOR the purpose of authorizing unaffiliated voters to request to affiliate with a political
4 party at an early voting center during a primary election and vote a ballot for the
5 voter’s preferred political party; and generally relating to affiliating with a political
6 party and voting in a primary election.
7 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
8 Article – Election Law
9 Section 3–303 and 3–305(a) and (e)
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
12 BY adding to
13 Article – Election Law
14 Section 3–305(e)
15 Annotated Code of Maryland
16 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
17 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
18 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
19 Article – Election Law
20 3–303.
21 (a) Notification of a change of party affiliation or a change to or from a decline
22 may be made:
23 (1) by information provided on a voter registration application by the same
24 methods provided for registration under Subtitle 2 of this title;

2 HOUSE BILL 1280
1 (2) by written notice, signed by the voter and sent by mail or otherwise
2 delivered to the local board in the county where the voter’s current voter registration
3 address is located or to which the voter has moved;
4 (3) by making application in person at the office of the local board in the
5 county where the voter’s current voter registration address is located or to which the voter
6 has moved;
7 (4) by information on a voter authority card or other appropriate form filled
8 out in a polling place; or
9 (5) by changing a name or address with the Motor Vehicle Administration.
10 (b) Party affiliation changes or changes to or from a decline:
11 (1) shall be processed at any time that registration is open; and
12 (2) except as provided in subsection (c) of this section, may not be processed
13 when registration is closed.
14 (c) (1) If a local board receives a request for a party affiliation change after the
15 close of registration, the local board shall make the change and it shall become effective for
16 the next election provided:
17 [(1)] (I) there is sufficient evidence, as determined by the local boards
18 pursuant to regulations adopted by the State Board, that the request was mailed on or
19 before the close of registration for that election; or
20 [(2)] (II) the request was submitted by the voter to the Motor Vehicle
21 Administration, a voter registration agency, another local board, or the State Board on or
22 before the close of registration for that election.
23 (2) DURING A PRIMARY ELECTION, A VOTER WHO HAS DECLINED TO
24 AFFILIATE WITH A POLITICAL PARTY MAY REQUEST TO AFFILIATE WITH A POLITICAL
25 PARTY AT AN EARLY VOTING CENTER.
26 3–305.
27 (a) (1) During early voting, an individual may appear in person at an early
28 voting center in the individual’s county of residence and apply to register to vote or change
29 the voter’s address on an existing voter registration.
30 (2) DURING EARLY VOTING BEFORE A PRIMARY ELECTION, AN
31 INDIVIDUAL WHO HAS DECLINED TO AFFILIATE WITH A POLITICAL PARTY MAY
32 APPEAR IN PERSON AT AN EARLY VOTING CENTER IN THE INDIVIDUAL’S COUNTY OF
HOUSE BILL 1280 3
1 RESIDENCE AND APPLY TO CHANGE THE VOTER’S EXISTING VOTER REGISTRATION
2 TO AFFILIATE WITH A POLITICAL PARTY.
3 (E) (1) WHEN A VOTER WHO HAS DECLINED TO AFFILIATE WITH A
4 POLITICAL PARTY APPLIES TO CHANGE THE VOTER’S EXISTING VOTER
5 REGISTRATION TO AFFILIATE WITH A POLITICAL PARTY DURING EARLY VOTING
6 BEFORE A PRIMARY ELECTION, THE ELECTION JUDGE SHALL DETERMINE WHETHER
7 THE VOTER RESIDES IN THE COUNTY IN WHICH THE VOTER SEEKS TO VOTE.
8 (2) IF THE VOTER IS A RESIDENT OF THE COUNTY, THE ELECTION
9 JUDGE SHALL:
10 (I) ISSUE THE VOTER A VOTER AUTHORITY CARD;
11 (II) HAVE THE VOTER SIGN THE VOTER AUTHORITY CARD; AND
12 (III) ISSUE THE VOTER A BALLOT FOR THE APPROPRIATE
13 POLITICAL PARTY.
14 [(e)] (F) The State Board shall adopt regulations and procedures in accordance
15 with the requirements of this section for the administration of voter registration during
16 early voting.
17 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
18 October 1, 2025.
