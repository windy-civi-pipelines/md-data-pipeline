A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Montgomery County – Stop Sign Monitoring Systems – Authorization
3 MC 4–25
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing the use of stop sign monitoring systems in school zones in
5 Montgomery County, if authorized by local law; establishing that certain provisions
6 of law relating to the inspection of recorded images apply to recorded images
7 produced by a stop sign monitoring system; providing that the owner or driver of a
8 motor vehicle recorded failing to obey a stop sign is subject to a citation and a certain
9 civil penalty under certain circumstances; establishing certain defenses to a charge
10 of an alleged violation recorded by a stop sign monitoring system; prohibiting a
11 contractor administering a stop sign monitoring system from being compensated in
12 a certain manner; and generally relating to the use of stop sign monitoring systems
13 in Montgomery County.
