L2 5lr2565
By: Delegates Toles, Harrison, Bartlett, Boafo, Harris, Holmes, and J. Long
Introduced and read first time: January 31, 2025
Assigned to: Environment and Transportation and Economic Matters
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Local Government – Charges for Paper Carryout Bags – Limitation
3 FOR the purpose of limiting the amount that retail establishments may charge for paper
4 carryout bags under certain local laws; and generally relating to local fees for paper
5 carryout bags.
6 BY adding to
7 Article – Local Government
8 Section 1–1321
9 Annotated Code of Maryland
10 (2013 Volume and 2024 Supplement)
11 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
12 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
13 Article – Local Government
14 1–1321.
15 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “RETAIL ESTABLISHMENT” MEANS A STORE, A FOOD
16 SERVICE FACILITY, OR ANY OTHER ESTABLISHMENT THAT PROVIDES BAGS TO ITS
17 CUSTOMERS AS A RESULT OF THE SALE OF A PRODUCT OR SERVICE, WHETHER
18 OPERATED FOR PROFIT OR NOT FOR PROFIT.
19 (B) THIS SECTION APPLIES TO ALL COUNTIES AND MUNICIPALITIES.
20 (C) IF A COUNTY OR MUNICIPALITY ENACTS A LOCAL LAW THAT
21 AUTHORIZES OR REQUIRES A RETAIL ESTABLISHMENT TO CHARGE FOR A PAPER
22 CARRYOUT BAG THAT IT PROVIDES TO A CUSTOMER, THE CHARGE MAY NOT EXCEED
23 10 CENTS FOR EACH PAPER CARRYOUT BAG.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.

2 HOUSE BILL 947
1 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
2 October 1, 2025.
