A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Wicomico County Board of Education – Election of Members, Student Member,
3 and Compensation
4 FOR the purpose of altering, beginning with a certain election, the districts from which
5 certain members of the Wicomico County Board of Education are elected; adding a
6 student member to the county board; providing that the members of the county board
7 are entitled to certain compensation and reimbursement; and generally relating to
8 the Wicomico County Board of Education.
9 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
10 Article – Education
11 Section 3–13A–01, 3–13A–02, 3–13A–03(a)(2), 3–13A–04, 3–13A–06, and 4–107(e)(7)
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
14 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
15 Article – Education
16 Section 3–13A–03(a)(1)
17 Annotated Code of Maryland
18 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
19 BY adding to
20 Article – Education
21 Section 3–13A–07 and 3–13A–08
22 Annotated Code of Maryland
23 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
24 BY repealing
25 Article – Education
26 Section 4–107(e)(6)
27 Annotated Code of Maryland

2 SENATE BILL 1032
1 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
2 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
3 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
4 Article – Education
5 3–13A–01.
6 (a) (1) The Wicomico County Board consists of [seven]:
7 (I) SEVEN nonpartisan elected voting members; AND
8 (II) ONE STUDENT MEMBER.
9 (2) [The] BEGINNING WITH THE GENERAL ELECTION IN 2026, THE
10 seven ELECTED members shall be elected [as follows:
11 (i) One member] from each of the [five] SEVEN councilmanic
12 districts in the county, elected by the voters of that district[; and
13 (ii) Two members at large, elected by the voters of the county].
14 (b) (1) A candidate who becomes an elected member of the county board must
15 be a resident and registered voter of Wicomico County.
16 (2) (i) An elected member who no longer resides in Wicomico County
17 may not continue as a member of the board.
18 (ii) A member elected from a councilmanic district who no longer
19 resides in that district may not continue as a member of the board.
20 (3) If the boundary line of a Wicomico County councilmanic district is
21 changed, the term of an incumbent ELECTED member of the county board who no longer
22 resides in that councilmanic district because of the change is not affected during this term.
23 3–13A–02.
24 (a) The seven ELECTED members of the Wicomico County Board shall be elected:
25 (1) At the general election in 2018 and at the general election every 4 years
26 thereafter; and
27 (2) In accordance with § 3–13A–01 of this subtitle and Title 8, Subtitle 8 of
28 the Election Law Article.
SENATE BILL 1032 3
1 (b) (1) The terms of the ELECTED members are as provided in this subsection.
2 (2) Each term of office begins on the first Monday in December after the
3 election of a member and until a successor is elected and qualifies.
4 (3) The term of office of each member is 4 years.
5 3–13A–03.
6 (a) (1) There is a Wicomico County School Board Nominating Commission.
7 (2) The purpose of the Commission is to select nominees to recommend to
8 the Wicomico County Council as qualified candidates for appointment to fill a vacancy OF
9 AN ELECTED MEMBER on the Wicomico County Board.
10 3–13A–04.
11 (a) Except as provided in subsection (b) of this section, the Wicomico County
12 Council shall appoint an individual to fill a vacancy OF AN ELECTED MEMBER on the
13 county board in accordance with § 3–13A–03 of this subtitle.
14 (b) (1) If a vacancy for [a] AN ELECTED member occurs before the date that is
15 30 days before the date for filing a certificate of candidacy for the primary election in the
16 presidential election year, the individual appointed under subsection (a) of this section shall
17 serve only until a successor is elected by the voters at the next general election.
18 (2) Candidates for the vacated office may be nominated at a primary
19 election in the same manner as for any other position on the county board.
20 3–13A–06.
21 At the first meeting of the county board in December of each year, the county board
22 shall elect a chair and vice chair from among the ELECTED members.
23 3–13A–07.
24 (A) THE STUDENT MEMBER OF THE COUNTY BOARD SHALL BE SELECTED IN
25 ACCORDANCE WITH PROCEDURES ESTABLISHED BY THE COUNTY BOARD.
26 (B) (1) THE STUDENT MEMBER SHALL:
27 (I) BE AN 11TH OR 12TH GRADE STUDENT IN GOOD STANDING
28 AND REGULARLY ENROLLED IN THE WICOMICO COUNTY PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM;
4 SENATE BILL 1032
1 (II) SERVE FOR 1 YEAR BEGINNING JULY 1 AFTER THE
2 SELECTION OF THE MEMBER;
3 (III) BE A NONVOTING MEMBER; AND
4 (IV) ADVISE THE COUNTY BOARD ON THE THOUGHTS AND
5 FEELINGS OF STUDENTS IN THE WICOMICO COUNTY PUBLIC SCHOOLS.
6 (2) UNLESS INVITED TO ATTEND BY AN AFFIRMATIVE VOTE OF A
7 MAJORITY OF THE ELECTED MEMBERS OF THE COUNTY BOARD, THE STUDENT
8 MEMBER MAY NOT ATTEND A CLOSED SESSION OF THE COUNTY BOARD.
9 3–13A–08.
10 (A) (1) THE CHAIR OF THE COUNTY BOARD IS ENTITLED TO RECEIVE
11 ANNUALLY AS COMPENSATION THE GREATER OF:
12 (I) $4,000; OR
13 (II) A SALARY SET BY THE WICOMICO COUNTY COUNCIL.
14 (2) THE VICE CHAIR OF THE COUNTY BOARD AND THE OTHER
15 ELECTED MEMBERS OF THE COUNTY BOARD ARE ENTITLED TO RECEIVE ANNUALLY
16 AS COMPENSATION THE GREATER OF:
17 (I) $3,700; OR
18 (II) A SALARY SET BY THE WICOMICO COUNTY COUNCIL.
19 (B) AFTER SUBMITTING VOUCHERS UNDER THE RULES AND REGULATIONS
20 ADOPTED BY THE COUNTY BOARD, THE ELECTED MEMBERS ARE ENTITLED TO
21 RECEIVE REIMBURSEMENT FOR TRAVEL AND OTHER EXPENSES AS PROVIDED IN
22 THE COUNTY BOARD’S BUDGET.
23 (C) (1) ON THE SUBMISSION OF EXPENSE VOUCHERS, THE STUDENT
24 MEMBER IS ENTITLED TO REIMBURSEMENT FOR TRAVEL AND OTHER EXPENSES
25 INCURRED IN THE PERFORMANCE OF OFFICIAL DUTIES FOR THE COUNTY BOARD.
26 (2) THE STUDENT MEMBER IS NOT ENTITLED TO ANY COMPENSATION
27 OR PAYMENT OTHER THAN THAT SPECIFIED UNDER PARAGRAPH (1) OF THIS
28 SUBSECTION FOR THE STUDENT MEMBER’S WORK AS THE STUDENT MEMBER OF THE
29 COUNTY BOARD.
