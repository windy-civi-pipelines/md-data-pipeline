1 AN ACT concerning
2 Department of Agriculture – Maryland Agricultural Commission and Young
3 Farmers
4 FOR the purpose of altering the membership of the Maryland Agricultural Commission;
5 establishing the Committee on Young Farmers for certain purposes, including to
6 communicate to the Maryland Agricultural Commission on certain matters relating
7 to young farmers in the State; repealing the provisions of law establishing and
8 governing the Young Farmers Advisory Board; and generally relating to the
9 Maryland Agricultural Commission and young farmers in the State.
