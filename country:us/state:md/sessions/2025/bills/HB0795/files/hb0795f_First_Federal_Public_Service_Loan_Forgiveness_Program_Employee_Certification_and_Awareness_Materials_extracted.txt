A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Federal Public Service Loan Forgiveness Program – Employee Certification and
3 Awareness Materials
4 FOR the purpose of establishing requirements related to the certification of employment
5 by public service employers under the federal Public Service Loan Forgiveness
6 Program, including requirements regarding determinations of whether employees
7 are full–time; requiring the Student Loan Ombudsman in the Office of the
8 Commissioner of Financial Regulation to develop and disseminate information to
9 increase awareness of and participation in the Program; and generally relating to
10 employment certification for the federal Public Service Loan Forgiveness Program.

12 Article – Financial Institutions
13 Section 2–104.1(a)(1) and (5)

19 (2) Providing information to the public, State agencies, elected officials,
20 and other individuals regarding student loan borrower problems and concerns; [and]

16 THE INFORMATION REQUIRED ON THE FEDERAL PUBLIC SERVICE LOAN

19 PUBLIC SERVICE EMPLOYER.

21 (E) “INSTITUTION OF HIGHER EDUCATION” HAS THE MEANING STATED IN §
22 10–101 OF THE EDUCATION ARTICLE.

24 FORGIVENESS PROGRAM ESTABLISHED UNDER 20 U.S.C. § 1087E(M).

26 STATE WHO IS DESIGNATED AS A QUALIFYING EMPLOYER UNDER THE FEDERAL
27 PUBLIC SERVICE LOAN FORGIVENESS PROGRAM, INCLUDING:
28 (I) A STATE OR LOCAL GOVERNMENT EMPLOYER, INCLUDING:

10 TRIBAL NATION GOVERNMENT ORGANIZATION, AGENCY, OR GOVERNMENTAL

5 (I) TO A PUBLIC SERVICE EMPLOYER THAT IS AN INSTITUTION
6 OF HIGHER EDUCATION; AND
7 (II) WITH RESPECT TO AN EMPLOYEE WHO IS AN ADJUNCT OR
8 TENURED PROFESSOR.

10 EMPLOYEE QUALIFIES AS FULL–TIME UNDER THE PROGRAM, A PUBLIC SERVICE

17 A PUBLIC SERVICE EMPLOYER’S POLICY OR ADJUSTMENT FOR ADDITIONAL WORK

19 (3) TO MAXIMIZE THE AMOUNT OF TIME FOR WHICH AN EMPLOYEE
20 CAN BE CONSIDERED TO BE A FULL–TIME EMPLOYEE, A PUBLIC SERVICE EMPLOYER

23 (I) THE HOURS ARE TAUGHT UNDER SEPARATE EMPLOYMENT
24 CONTRACTS; OR
25 (II) THE ACADEMIC TERMS ARE SEPARATED BY ROUTINE
26 ACADEMIC VACATION.

17 SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION WITHIN 30 DAYS AFTER THE EMPLOYEE’S FIRST

20 DEVELOPED UNDER SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION BY MAIL, BY E–MAIL, OR

22 (D) ON OR AFTER MARCH 15, 2026, EACH PUBLIC SERVICE EMPLOYER
23 SHALL ANNUALLY PROVIDE THE MOST RECENTLY AVAILABLE VERSION OF THE
24 MATERIALS DEVELOPED UNDER SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION TO ALL
25 EMPLOYEES IN WRITTEN OR ELECTRONIC FORM.
