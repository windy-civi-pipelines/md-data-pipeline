A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Carroll County – Natural Resources – Sunday Hunting
3 FOR the purpose of authorizing a person to hunt in Carroll County on each Sunday of the
4 game bird and game mammal seasons; and generally relating to Sunday hunting in
5 Carroll County.
6 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
7 Article – Natural Resources
8 Section 10–410(a)
9 Annotated Code of Maryland
10 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
11 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
12 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
13 Article – Natural Resources
14 10–410.
15 (a) (1) (i) The Department may allow a person to hunt on each Sunday of
16 the game bird and game mammal seasons in:
17 1. Allegany County;
18 2. Subject to subparagraph (iii) of this paragraph, Calvert
19 County, from 30 minutes before sunrise until 10:30 a.m.;
20 3. Subject to subparagraph (iii) of this paragraph, Caroline
21 County, from 30 minutes before sunrise until 10:30 a.m.;

17 (7) Notwithstanding any other provision of this subtitle, the Department
18 may allow a person with a deer management permit to shoot deer on any Sunday
19 throughout the year in accordance with the terms of the permit.
20 (8) The Department may allow a person using a State certified raptor to
21 hunt game birds or mammals on each Sunday during the open season for the game birds
22 or mammals.
23 (9) The Department may allow an unarmed person participating in an
24 organized fox chase to chase foxes on Sunday.
25 (10) The Department may allow a person shooting pen–reared game birds
26 or tower–released flighted mallard ducks to hunt on Sunday at a regulated shooting ground
27 in accordance with § 10–906 of this title.
28 (11) The Department may allow a person who is 16 years old or younger to
29 hunt on Sunday if participating in a junior hunt established by the Department in
30 accordance with this subtitle.
