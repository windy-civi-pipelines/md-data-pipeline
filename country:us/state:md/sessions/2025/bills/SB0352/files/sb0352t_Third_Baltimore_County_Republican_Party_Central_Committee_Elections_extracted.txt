CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Baltimore County – Republican Party Central Committee – Elections
3 FOR the purpose of altering the number of members elected from each councilmanic district
4 of Baltimore County to the Baltimore County Republican Party Central Committee;
5 and generally relating to the Baltimore County Republican Party Central
6 Committee.
7 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
8 Article – Election Law
9 Section 4–203(c)(1)
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
14 Article – Election Law
15 4–203.
16 (c) (1) In Baltimore County, the Republican Party Central Committee shall
17 consist of [four] THREE members elected from each councilmanic district in the county.
18 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
19 October 1, 2025.
