12 County business community;

15 and
16 (iii) Three members appointed by the Secretary, one of whom shall
17 be a current slip holder at Somers Cove Marina.
18 (3) (i) The term of a member is 4 years.
19 (ii) A member may not serve more than 2 consecutive terms.
20 (iii) At the end of a term, a member continues to serve until a
21 successor is appointed and qualifies.
22 (iv) A member who is appointed after a term has begun serves only
23 for the rest of the term and until a successor is appointed and qualifies.
24 (4) A member is not entitled to compensation except for reimbursement for
25 expenses as provided in the budget of the Commission.
26 (5) (i) The Commission shall elect a chair and a vice chair of the
27 Commission from among its members.
28 (ii) The Commission shall appoint a secretary–treasurer who need
29 not be a member of the Commission.

7 (7) Five members of the Commission are a quorum.
8 (8) A majority vote of the members present at a meeting having a quorum
9 is needed for the Commission to act.

11 reason upon a majority vote of the members of the Commission.
12 (ii) If a member is removed, a new member shall promptly be
13 appointed by the individual or body that appointed the removed member.
