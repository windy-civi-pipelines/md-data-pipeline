E4 5lr2821
HB 533/24 – JUD
By: Delegate Davis
Introduced and read first time: January 29, 2025
Assigned to: Judiciary
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 County Police Accountability Boards – Investigation of Complaints of Police
3 Misconduct
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing the local governing body of a county to authorize, by local
5 law, the county’s police accountability board to exercise investigatory and subpoena
6 powers; authorizing a police accountability board to conduct an investigation of
7 police misconduct concurrently with a law enforcement agency investigating the
8 complaint; and generally relating to county police accountability boards.
9 BY adding to
10 Article – Public Safety
11 Section 3–102(e)
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
14 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
16 Article – Public Safety
17 3–102.
18 (E) (1) SUBJECT TO PARAGRAPH (2) OF THIS SUBSECTION, A LOCAL
19 GOVERNING BODY OF A COUNTY, INCLUDING BALTIMORE CITY, MAY BY LOCAL LAW
20 AUTHORIZE ITS POLICE ACCOUNTABILITY BOARD TO EXERCISE INVESTIGATORY
21 AND SUBPOENA POWERS.
22 (2) A POLICE ACCOUNTABILITY BOARD MAY CONDUCT AN
23 INVESTIGATION OF A COMPLAINT OF POLICE MISCONDUCT UNDER THIS
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb0836*
2 HOUSE BILL 836
1 SUBSECTION CONCURRENTLY WITH A LAW ENFORCEMENT AGENCY INVESTIGATING
2 THE COMPLAINT.
3 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
4 October 1, 2025.
