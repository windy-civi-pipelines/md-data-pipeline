1 AN ACT concerning
2 Procurement – Employee Stock Ownership Plan Preference – Pilot
3 FOR the purpose of establishing a preference program for certain procurements by
4 authorizing the Maryland Stadium Authority, the University System of Maryland,
5 Morgan State University, St. Mary’s College of Maryland, and Baltimore City
6 Community College to establish a certain preference program for certain
7 procurements; establishing authorizing a certain price preference for bids and
8 proposals by an entity that utilizes certain employee stock ownership plans;
9 requiring contractors to provide certain information to a unit regarding the use of an
10 employee stock ownership plan; and generally relating to procurement preferences
11 for entities that utilize an employee stock ownership plan.
