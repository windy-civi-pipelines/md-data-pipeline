Assigned to: Ways and Means
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public Schools – Sexually Explicit Materials – Prohibited in Libraries and
3 Media Centers
4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting sexually explicit materials in public elementary, middle,
5 and secondary school libraries and media centers; and generally relating to sexually
6 explicit material in public school libraries and media centers.
7 BY adding to
8 Article – Education
9 Section 7–136
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
14 Article – Education
15 7–136.
16 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “SEXUALLY EXPLICIT MATERIALS” MEANS BOOKS AND
17 AUDITORY AND VISUAL MATERIALS THAT CONTAIN GRAPHIC OR OBSCENE
18 DEPICTIONS OF SEXUAL ACTIVITY THAT ARE NOT:
19 (1) AGE–APPROPRIATE; OR
20 (2) USED AS PART OF THE APPROVED INSTRUCTIONAL MATERIALS
21 FOR INSTRUCTION ON FAMILY LIFE AND HUMAN SEXUALITY AS PART OF THE
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb0282*
2 HOUSE BILL 282
1 COMPREHENSIVE HEALTH EDUCATION PROGRAM REQUIRED UNDER THE
2 MARYLAND HEALTH EDUCATION STANDARDS.
3 (B) A PUBLIC ELEMENTARY, MIDDLE, OR SECONDARY SCHOOL LIBRARY OR
4 MEDIA CENTER MAY NOT CONTAIN SEXUALLY EXPLICIT MATERIALS.
5 (C) EACH COUNTY BOARD SHALL ADOPT POLICIES AND PROCEDURES TO
6 IMPLEMENT THIS SECTION.
7 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
8 1, 2025.
