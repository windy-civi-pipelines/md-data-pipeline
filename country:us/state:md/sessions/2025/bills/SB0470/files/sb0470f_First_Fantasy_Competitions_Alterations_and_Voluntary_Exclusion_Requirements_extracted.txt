Assigned to: Budget and Taxation
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Fantasy Competitions – Alterations and Voluntary Exclusion Requirements
3 FOR the purpose of altering the definition of “fantasy competition” for purposes of
4 provisions of law governing fantasy competitions to include certain competitions;
5 requiring the State Lottery and Gaming Control Commission to establish a certain
6 voluntary exclusion program; prohibiting a fantasy competition operator from
7 permitting an individual on the mandatory exclusion list to establish a fantasy
8 competition account or participate in fantasy competitions; prohibiting a fantasy
9 competition operator from paying a prize or an award to certain excluded individuals;
10 altering the definitions of “sporting event” and “sports wagering” for purposes of
11 provisions of law governing the conduct of sports wagering; and generally relating to
12 fantasy competitions and sports wagering.
13 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
14 Article – State Government
15 Section 9–1D–01(a), (c), (e), and (f) and 9–1E–01(a)
16 Annotated Code of Maryland
17 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
18 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
19 Article – State Government
20 Section 9–1D–01(d), 9–1D–02, 9–1D–05, and 9–1E–01(i) and (j)
21 Annotated Code of Maryland
22 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
23 BY adding to
24 Article – State Government
25 Section 9–1D–05
26 Annotated Code of Maryland
27 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)

18 INDIVIDUAL’S TRANSACTION HISTORY.

23 (2) THE DATE OF THE PARTICIPATION;
24 (3) THE AMOUNT OR VALUE OF ANY MONEY, PRIZES, OR AWARDS
25 FORFEITED, IF ANY; AND
26 (4) ANY OTHER ACTION TAKEN BY THE FANTASY COMPETITION
27 OPERATOR.
28 (E) (1) A FANTASY COMPETITION OPERATOR MAY NOT PAY ANY PRIZE OR
29 AWARD TO AN INDIVIDUAL WHO IS ON THE COMMISSION’S SELF–EXCLUSION LIST.

18 (i) a professional sports or athletic event;

20 (iii) an Olympic or international sports or athletic event;

27 1. the horse racing licensee of the applicable breed;
28 2. the organization representing the majority of the owners
29 and trainers of the applicable breed in the State;

1 3. the organization representing the majority of the
2 applicable breeders in the State; and
3 4. the State Racing Commission;
4 (vii) EXCEPT AS PROVIDED IN SUBTITLE 1D OF THIS TITLE, any
5 portion of a sporting event, including the individual performance statistics of athletes or
6 competitors in a sporting event; or
7 (viii) an award event or competition of national or international
8 prominence if expressly authorized by the Commission.
9 (2) “Sporting event” does not include:
10 (i) a high school sports or athletic event; or
11 (ii) a fantasy competition regulated under Subtitle 1D of this title.
12 (j) (1) “Sports wagering” means the business of accepting wagers on any
13 sporting event by any system or method of wagering, including single–game bets, teaser
14 bets, parlays, over–under, moneyline, pools, exchange wagering, in–game wagering,
15 in–play bets, proposition bets, and straight bets.
16 (2) “SPORTS WAGERING” DOES NOT INCLUDE A FANTASY
17 COMPETITION CONDUCTED IN COMPLIANCE WITH SUBTITLE 1D OF THIS TITLE.

19 October 1, 2025.
