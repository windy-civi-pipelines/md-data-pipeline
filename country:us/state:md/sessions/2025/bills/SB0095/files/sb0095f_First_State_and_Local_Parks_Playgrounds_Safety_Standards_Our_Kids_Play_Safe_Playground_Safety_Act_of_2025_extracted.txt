Section 1:
M1, O4 5lr1252
SB 886/24 – EEE (PRE–FILED)
By: Senator Folden
Requested: October 17, 2024
Introduced and read first time: January 8, 2025
Assigned to: Education, Energy, and the Environment
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 State and Local Parks – Playgrounds – Safety Standards
3 (Our Kids Play Safe Playground Safety Act of 2025)
4 FOR the purpose of requiring each unit of State or local government responsible for the
5 construction and maintenance of a playground to ensure that the playground is
6 constructed and maintained in accordance with certain safety standards; requiring
7 a unit of State or local government to inspect a certain playground to evaluate
8 compliance with certain safety standards and to keep an inspection record in a
9 certain manner; and generally relating to safety standards for playgrounds.
10 BY adding to
11 Article – Natural Resources
12 Section 5–2101 and 5–2102 to be under the new subtitle “Subtitle 21. Playground
13 Safety”
14 Annotated Code of Maryland
15 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
16 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
17 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
18 Article – Natural Resources
19 SUBTITLE 21. PLAYGROUND SAFETY.
20 5–2101.
21 IN THIS SUBTITLE, “PLAYGROUND” MEANS A PUBLIC OUTDOOR RECREATION
22 AREA FOR CHILDREN EQUIPPED WITH ONE OR MORE PLAY STRUCTURES.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *sb0095*
2 SENATE BILL 95
1 5–2102.
2 (A) EACH UNIT OF STATE OR LOCAL GOVERNMENT RESPONSIBLE FOR THE
3 CONSTRUCTION AND MAINTENANCE OF A PLAYGROUND SHALL ENSURE THAT THE
4 PLAYGROUND IS CONSTRUCTED AND MAINTAINED IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE MOST
5 RECENT PUBLICATION OF:
6 (1) THE PUBLIC PLAYGROUND SAFETY HANDBOOK, PUBLISHED BY
7 THE U.S. CONSUMER PRODUCT SAFETY COMMISSION;
8 (2) THE STANDARD CONSUMER SAFETY PERFORMANCE
9 SPECIFICATION FOR PLAYGROUND EQUIPMENT FOR PUBLIC USE, PUBLISHED BY
10 THE AMERICAN NATIONAL STANDARDS INSTITUTE; AND
11 (3) THE STANDARD SPECIFICATION FOR IMPACT ATTENUATION OF
12 SURFACING MATERIALS WITHIN THE USE ZONE OF PLAYGROUND EQUIPMENT,
13 PUBLISHED BY THE AMERICAN NATIONAL STANDARDS INSTITUTE.
14 (B) AT LEAST ONCE EVERY 2 YEARS, EACH UNIT OF STATE OR LOCAL
15 GOVERNMENT RESPONSIBLE FOR A PLAYGROUND SHALL INSPECT THE
16 PLAYGROUND TO EVALUATE COMPLIANCE WITH SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION.
17 (C) AFTER COMPLETION OF EACH INSPECTION CONDUCTED IN
18 ACCORDANCE WITH SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION, A UNIT OF STATE OR LOCAL
19 GOVERNMENT SHALL KEEP AN INSPECTION RECORD, INCLUDING A WRITTEN PLAN
20 TO ADDRESS ANY SAFETY HAZARDS DISCOVERED DURING THE INSPECTION.
21 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
22 October 1, 2025.
