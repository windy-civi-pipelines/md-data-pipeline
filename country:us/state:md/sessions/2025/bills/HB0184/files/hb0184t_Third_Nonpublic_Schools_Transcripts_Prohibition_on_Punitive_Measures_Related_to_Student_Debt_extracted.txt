CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Nonpublic Schools – Transcripts – Prohibition on Punitive Measures Related to
3 Student Debt
4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting nonpublic schools that participate in State–funded
5 education programs from refusing to issue a transcript to a current or former student
6 or the student’s parent or guardian or taking other punitive measures regarding a
7 student’s transcript request because the student owes a debt to the nonpublic school;
8 and generally relating to the issuance of transcripts by nonpublic schools.
9 BY adding to
10 Article – Education
11 Section 7–118
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
14 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
16 Article – Education
17 7–118.
18 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
19 INDICATED.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from existing law.
Underlining indicates amendments to bill.
Strike out indicates matter stricken from the bill by amendment or deleted from the law by
amendment. *hb0184*
2 HOUSE BILL 184
1 (2) (I) “DEBT” MEANS ANY MONEY, OBLIGATION, CLAIM, OR SUM
2 DUE OR OWED BY A STUDENT OR FORMER STUDENT TO A NONPUBLIC SCHOOL.
3 (II) “DEBT” DOES NOT INCLUDE THE FEE, IF ANY, CHARGED TO
4 A STUDENT FOR THE ACTUAL COSTS OF PROVIDING A TRANSCRIPT.
5 (3) “NONPUBLIC SCHOOL” MEANS A NONPUBLIC SCHOOL IN THE
6 STATE THAT PARTICIPATES IN STATE–FUNDED EDUCATION PROGRAMS.
7 (4) “STUDENT” INCLUDES CURRENT STUDENTS AND FORMER
8 STUDENTS OF A NONPUBLIC SCHOOL.
9 (B) A NONPUBLIC SCHOOL MAY NOT:
10 (1) REFUSE TO ISSUE A TRANSCRIPT TO A STUDENT OR THE PARENT
11 OR GUARDIAN OF A STUDENT BECAUSE THE STUDENT OWES A DEBT;
12 (2) CONDITION THE ISSUANCE OF A TRANSCRIPT ON THE PAYMENT
13 OF A DEBT;
14 (3) CHARGE A HIGHER FEE FOR OBTAINING A TRANSCRIPT BECAUSE
15 THE STUDENT OWES A DEBT;
16 (4) PROVIDE LESS FAVORABLE TREATMENT OF A TRANSCRIPT
17 REQUEST BECAUSE A STUDENT OWES A DEBT; OR
18 (5) USE TRANSCRIPT ISSUANCE AS A TOOL OF DEBT COLLECTION.
19 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
20 1, 2025.
Approved:

Governor.

Speaker of the House of Delegates.

President of the Senate.
