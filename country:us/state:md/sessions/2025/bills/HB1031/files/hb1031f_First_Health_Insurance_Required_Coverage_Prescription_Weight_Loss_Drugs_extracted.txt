Section 1:
J5 5lr3321
By: Delegates Miller, Acevero, Alston, Rose, Schmidt, Tomlinson, and Valentine
Introduced and read first time: February 3, 2025
Assigned to: Health and Government Operations
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Health Insurance – Required Coverage – Prescription Weight Loss Drugs
3 FOR the purpose of requiring certain insurers, nonprofit health service plans, and health
4 maintenance organizations to provide coverage for prescription weight loss drugs for
5 certain individuals who have been diagnosed with obesity; and generally relating to
6 the coverage of prescription weight loss drugs by health insurance carriers.
7 BY adding to
8 Article – Insurance
9 Section 15–839.1
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2017 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
14 Article – Insurance
15 15–839.1.
16 (A) THIS SECTION APPLIES TO:
17 (1) INSURERS AND NONPROFIT HEALTH SERVICE PLANS THAT
18 PROVIDE COVERAGE FOR PRESCRIPTION DRUGS UNDER INDIVIDUAL, GROUP, OR
19 BLANKET HEALTH INSURANCE POLICIES OR CONTRACTS THAT ARE ISSUED OR
20 DELIVERED IN THE STATE; AND
21 (2) HEALTH MAINTENANCE ORGANIZATIONS THAT PROVIDE
22 COVERAGE FOR PRESCRIPTION DRUGS UNDER INDIVIDUAL OR GROUP CONTRACTS
23 THAT ARE ISSUED OR DELIVERED IN THE STATE.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb1031*
2 HOUSE BILL 1031
1 (B) AN ENTITY SUBJECT TO THIS SECTION SHALL PROVIDE COVERAGE FOR
2 PRESCRIPTION WEIGHT LOSS DRUGS FOR INDIVIDUALS WHO HAVE BEEN
3 DIAGNOSED WITH OBESITY AND AT LEAST ONE COMORBID MEDICAL CONDITION
4 THAT CAN BE TREATED WITH PRESCRIPTION WEIGHT LOSS DRUGS.
5 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall apply to all
6 policies, contracts, and health benefit plans issued, delivered, or renewed in the State on or
7 after January 1, 2026.
8 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
9 January 1, 2026.
