A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Harford County – Alcoholic Beverages – Class AF (Athletic Field) License
3 FOR the purpose of establishing a 6–day and a 7–day Class AF (athletic field) beer and
4 wine license in Harford County; authorizing the Board of License Commissioners for
5 Harford County to issue the 6–day or 7–day license only to a person who leases, owns,
6 or operates an athletic field for profit; and generally relating to alcoholic beverages
7 licenses in Harford County.
8 BY renumbering
9 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
10 Section 22–1001 and 22–1001.1
11 to be Section 22–1001.1 and 22–1001.2, respectively
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2024 Replacement Volume)
14 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
15 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
16 Section 22–102
17 Annotated Code of Maryland
18 (2024 Replacement Volume)
19 BY adding to
20 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
21 Section 22–1001
22 Annotated Code of Maryland
23 (2024 Replacement Volume)
24 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
25 That Section(s) 22–1001 and 22–1001.1 of Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis of
26 the Annotated Code of Maryland be renumbered to be Section(s) 22–1001.1 and 22–1001.2,
27 respectively.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *sb1015*
2 SENATE BILL 1015
1 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That the Laws of Maryland read
2 as follows:
3 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
4 22–102.
5 This title applies only in Harford County.
6 22–1001.
7 (A) THERE IS:
8 (1) A 6–DAY CLASS AF (ATHLETIC FIELD) BEER AND WINE LICENSE;
9 AND
10 (2) A 7–DAY CLASS AF (ATHLETIC FIELD) BEER AND WINE LICENSE.
11 (B) THE BOARD MAY ISSUE THE 6–DAY OR THE 7–DAY LICENSE ONLY TO A
12 PERSON WHO LEASES, OWNS, OR OPERATES AN ATHLETIC FIELD FOR PROFIT.
13 (C) (1) A 6–DAY LICENSE AUTHORIZES THE LICENSE HOLDER TO SELL
14 BEER AND WINE FOR ON–PREMISES CONSUMPTION MONDAY THROUGH SATURDAY,
15 FROM 12 P.M. TO 9 P.M.
16 (2) A 7–DAY LICENSE AUTHORIZES THE LICENSE HOLDER TO SELL
17 BEER AND WINE FOR ON–PREMISES CONSUMPTION MONDAY THROUGH SUNDAY,
18 FROM 12 P.M. TO 9 P.M.
19 (D) A LICENSE HOLDER SHALL PURCHASE BEER AND WINE FROM AN
20 AUTHORIZED AND LICENSED WHOLESALER.
21 (E) A LICENSE HOLDER SHALL PROVIDE PREPACKAGED FOOD OR SNACKS
22 AT ALL TIMES WHEN BEER AND WINE ARE SOLD.
23 (F) THE ANNUAL LICENSE FEES ARE:
24 (1) $1,300 FOR A 6–DAY LICENSE; AND
25 (2) $1,400 FOR A 7–DAY LICENSE.
26 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
27 1, 2025.
