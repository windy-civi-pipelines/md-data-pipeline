1 AN ACT concerning
2 State Government – Interdepartmental Advisory Committee on Office of Small,
3 Minority, and Women Business Affairs – Repeal of Interdepartmental Advisory
4 Committee – Repeal and Definitions and Establishment of the Governor’s
5 Subcabinet on Socioeconomic Procurement Participation
6 FOR the purpose of repealing the Interdepartmental Advisory Committee on Small,
7 Minority, and Women Business Affairs within the Office of Small, Minority, and
8 Women Business Affairs; establishing the Governor’s Subcabinet on Socioeconomic
9 Procurement Participation to study and make recommendations on socioeconomic
10 procurement in the State; requiring the Subcabinet to conduct certain meetings and
11 report annually to the Governor altering the definition of “minority person” for the
12 purpose of certain provisions of law governing the Office; and generally relating to
13 the Interdepartmental Advisory Committee on Office of Small, Minority, and Women
14 Business Affairs.
