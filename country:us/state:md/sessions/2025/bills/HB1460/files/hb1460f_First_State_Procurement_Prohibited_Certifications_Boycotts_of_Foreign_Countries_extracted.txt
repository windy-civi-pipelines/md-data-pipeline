Section 1:
P2 5lr3002
By: Delegates Young, Acevero, Alston, and Martinez
Introduced and read first time: February 7, 2025
Assigned to: Health and Government Operations
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 State Procurement – Prohibited Certifications – Boycotts of Foreign Countries
3 FOR the purpose of prohibiting a procurement officer from requiring a certain certification
4 from a bidder or offeror or including a certain clause in a State procurement contract
5 or contract renewal relating to boycotts of foreign countries; and generally relating
6 to prohibited certifications and State procurement.
7 BY adding to
8 Article – State Finance and Procurement
9 Section 13–212.2 and 13–228
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
14 Article – State Finance and Procurement
15 13–212.2.
16 A PROCUREMENT OFFICER MAY NOT REQUIRE A BIDDER OR OFFEROR, AS A
17 CONDITION TO SUBMITTING A BID OR PROPOSAL ON A STATE PROCUREMENT, TO
18 CERTIFY THAT THE BIDDER OR OFFEROR, EXCEPT AS REQUIRED UNDER THIS
19 ARTICLE OR UNDER FEDERAL LAW:
20 (1) IS NOT ENGAGING IN A BOYCOTT OF A FOREIGN COUNTRY; OR
21 (2) WILL REFRAIN FROM A BOYCOTT OF A FOREIGN COUNTRY FOR
22 THE DURATION OF THE CONTRACTUAL OBLIGATIONS.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
