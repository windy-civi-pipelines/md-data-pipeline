By: Senator Klausmeier
Requested: September 27, 2024
Introduced and read first time: January 8, 2025
Assigned to: Education, Energy, and the Environment
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 State Designations – Symbol of Remembrance for September 11 – Freedom Flag
3 FOR the purpose of designating the Freedom Flag as the State symbol of remembrance for
4 the attacks of September 11; and generally relating to a State symbol of
5 remembrance for the attacks of September 11.
6 BY renumbering
7 Article – General Provisions
8 Section 7–319
9 to be Section 7–320
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
12 BY adding to
13 Article – General Provisions
14 Section 7–319
15 Annotated Code of Maryland
16 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
17 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
18 That Section(s) 7–319 of Article – General Provisions of the Annotated Code of Maryland
19 be renumbered to be Section(s) 7–320.
20 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That the Laws of Maryland read
21 as follows:
22 Article – General Provisions
23 7–319.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.

2 SENATE BILL 169
1 THE FREEDOM FLAG, DESIGNED AND CREATED BY RICHARD NICHOLAS
2 MELITO OF RICHMOND, VIRGINIA, TO MEMORIALIZE THE VICTIMS OF THE EVENTS
3 OF SEPTEMBER 11, 2001, IS THE STATE SYMBOL OF REMEMBRANCE FOR THE
4 ATTACKS OF SEPTEMBER 11.
5 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
6 October 1, 2025.
