R5 5lr1231
By: Delegates T. Morgan, Crosby, and M. Morgan
Introduced and read first time: January 20, 2025
Assigned to: Environment and Transportation
Committee Report: Favorable with amendments
House action: Adopted
Read second time: March 21, 2025
CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 St. Mary’s County – Vehicle Laws – Prohibition on Roadway Solicitations
3 FOR the purpose of prohibiting a person in St. Mary’s County from standing in a roadway,
4 median divider, or intersection to solicit in an aggressive manner money or donations
5 from the occupant of a vehicle; and generally relating to roadway solicitations in St.
6 Mary’s County.
7 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments, adding to
8 Article – Transportation
9 Section 21–507(c) 21–507.1
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
14 Article – Transportation
15 21–507.
16 (c) In Calvert County, Carroll County, Charles County, Harford County, ST.
17 MARY’S COUNTY, and Washington County, a person may not stand in a roadway, median
18 divider, or intersection to solicit money or donations of any kind from the occupant of a
19 vehicle.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from existing law.
Underlining indicates amendments to bill.
Strike out indicates matter stricken from the bill by amendment or deleted from the law by
amendment. *hb0460*
2 HOUSE BILL 460
1 21–507.1.
2 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “AGGRESSIVE MANNER” MEANS:
3 (1) INTENTIONALLY BLOCKING OR INTERFERING WITH THE SAFE OR
4 FREE PASSAGE OF A PEDESTRIAN OR VEHICLE BY ANY MEANS, INCLUDING
5 UNREASONABLY CAUSING A PEDESTRIAN OR DRIVER TO TAKE EVASIVE ACTION TO
6 AVOID PHYSICAL CONTACT; OR
7 (2) USING VIOLENT OR THREATENING GESTURES TOWARD AN
8 INDIVIDUAL.
9 (B) IN ST. MARY’S COUNTY, A PERSON MAY NOT STAND IN A ROADWAY,
10 MEDIAN DIVIDER, OR INTERSECTION TO SOLICIT IN AN AGGRESSIVE MANNER
11 MONEY OR DONATIONS OF ANY KIND FROM AN OCCUPANT OF A VEHICLE.
12 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
13 October 1, 2025.
Approved:
