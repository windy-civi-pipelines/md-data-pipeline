By: Delegate Mangione
Requested: June 7, 2024
Introduced and read first time: January 8, 2025
Assigned to: Health and Government Operations
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 General Provisions – Commemorative Months – Italian Heritage Month
3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Governor annually to proclaim a certain month as Italian
4 Heritage Month; requiring the proclamation to urge certain organizations to observe
5 Italian Heritage Month properly; and generally relating to Italian Heritage Month.
6 BY renumbering
7 Article – General Provisions
8 Section 7–507
9 to be Section 7–508
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
12 BY adding to
13 Article – General Provisions
14 Section 7–507
15 Annotated Code of Maryland
16 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
17 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
18 That Section(s) 7–507 of Article – General Provisions of the Annotated Code of Maryland
19 be renumbered to be Section(s) 7–508.
20 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That the Laws of Maryland read
21 as follows:
22 Article – General Provisions
23 7–507.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb0117*
2 HOUSE BILL 117
1 (A) THE GOVERNOR ANNUALLY SHALL PROCLAIM THE MONTH OF OCTOBER
2 AS ITALIAN HERITAGE MONTH IN RECOGNITION OF THE CONTRIBUTIONS THAT
3 ITALIAN AMERICANS HAVE MADE TO THE STATE.
4 (B) THE PROCLAMATION SHALL URGE EDUCATIONAL AND CULTURAL
5 ORGANIZATIONS TO OBSERVE ITALIAN HERITAGE MONTH PROPERLY WITH
6 APPROPRIATE PROGRAMS, CEREMONIES, AND ACTIVITIES.
7 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
8 1, 2025.
