Introduced and read first time: February 6, 2025
Assigned to: Environment and Transportation
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Vehicle Emissions Inspection Program – Exemptions – Individuals With
3 Disabilities and Older Drivers
4 FOR the purpose of increasing the maximum number of miles that a motor vehicle
5 registered to an individual with a disability or owned by an individual who is at least
6 a certain age may be driven annually to be eligible for an exemption from certain
7 mandatory vehicle emissions inspections; and generally relating to the vehicle
8 emissions inspection program.
9 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
10 Article – Transportation
11 Section 23–206.2(a) and (b)
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
14 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
16 Article – Transportation
17 23–206.2.
18 (a) (1) A motor vehicle for which special registration plates have been issued
19 under § 13–616 of this article is exempt from the mandatory inspections required by this
20 subtitle if:
21 (i) All of the owners of the motor vehicle meet the disability
22 requirements of § 13–616(b)(1) of this article;

1 (ii) The motor vehicle is driven [5,000] 10,000 miles or less
2 annually; and
3 (iii) The exemption is not otherwise prohibited by federal law.
4 (2) In order to qualify for an exemption under paragraph (1) of this
5 subsection, all owners of the motor vehicle shall certify the following:
6 (i) That the owner of the motor vehicle meets the disability
7 requirements of § 13–616(b)(1) of this article;
8 (ii) That the motor vehicle has been issued a special disabled
9 person’s registration number and special registration plates under § 13–616 of this article;
10 (iii) That the motor vehicle is driven [5,000] 10,000 miles or less
11 annually; and
12 (iv) The motor vehicle’s odometer reading at the time of the
13 certification.
14 (3) The certification required in paragraph (2) of this subsection shall be
15 made on a form provided by the Administration.
16 (b) (1) A motor vehicle owned by an individual who is at least 70 years of age
17 at the time of a scheduled mandatory inspection under this subtitle is exempt from the
18 mandatory inspections required by this subtitle if:
19 (i) All of the owners of the motor vehicle are at least 70 years of age
20 at the time of the scheduled mandatory inspection under this subtitle;
21 (ii) The motor vehicle is being driven [5,000] 10,000 miles or less
22 annually; and
23 (iii) The exemption is not otherwise prohibited by federal law.
24 (2) In order to qualify for an exemption under paragraph (1) of this
25 subsection, all owners of the motor vehicle shall certify the following:
26 (i) That all of the owners of the motor vehicle are at least 70 years
27 of age at the time of a scheduled mandatory inspection under this subtitle;
28 (ii) That the motor vehicle is being driven [5,000] 10,000 miles or
29 less annually; and
30 (iii) The motor vehicle’s odometer reading at the time of the
31 certification.
