A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 County Boards of Education – Student Cellular Phone Use Policy –
3 Establishment
4 FOR the purpose of requiring each county board of education to develop and implement a
5 policy limiting the use of a cellular phone by a student during the school day except
6 under certain circumstances; and generally relating to county boards of education
7 and a student cellular phone use policy.
8 BY adding to
9 Article – Education
10 Section 7–136
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
15 Article – Education
16 7–136.
17 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “INSTRUCTIONAL TIME” MEANS ANY TIME DURING
18 THE SCHOOL DAY, EXCEPT A STUDENT’S LUNCH PERIOD.
19 (B) EACH COUNTY BOARD SHALL DEVELOP AND IMPLEMENT A POLICY TO
20 LIMIT THE USE OF CELLULAR PHONES BY STUDENTS DURING INSTRUCTIONAL TIME.
21 (C) EXCEPT AS PROVIDED IN SUBSECTION (D) OF THIS SECTION, THE
22 POLICY DEVELOPED UNDER THIS SECTION SHALL:
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb0057*
2 HOUSE BILL 57
1 (1) PROHIBIT A STUDENT FROM USING A CELLULAR PHONE DURING
2 INSTRUCTIONAL TIME; AND
3 (2) REQUIRE A STUDENT TO STORE THE STUDENT’S CELLULAR
4 PHONE IN A SECURE PLACE DURING INSTRUCTIONAL TIME, INCLUDING:
5 (I) A LOCKER;
6 (II) A CLOSED BACKPACK; OR
7 (III) A STORAGE CONTAINER PROVIDED BY THE SCHOOL.
8 (D) THE POLICY DEVELOPED UNDER THIS SECTION MAY NOT PROHIBIT A
9 STUDENT FROM USING A CELLULAR PHONE:
10 (1) FOR ANY PURPOSE DOCUMENTED IN THE STUDENT’S
11 INDIVIDUALIZED EDUCATION PROGRAM;
12 (2) TO MONITOR OR ADDRESS A STUDENT’S DOCUMENTED HEALTH
13 ISSUE; OR
14 (3) WHEN DIRECTED BY AN EDUCATOR OR ADMINISTRATOR FOR
15 EDUCATIONAL PURPOSES.
16 (E) A POLICY DEVELOPED UNDER THIS SECTION SHALL INCLUDE
17 APPROPRIATE DISCIPLINARY MEASURES FOR VIOLATIONS OF THE POLICY.
18 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
19 1, 2025.
