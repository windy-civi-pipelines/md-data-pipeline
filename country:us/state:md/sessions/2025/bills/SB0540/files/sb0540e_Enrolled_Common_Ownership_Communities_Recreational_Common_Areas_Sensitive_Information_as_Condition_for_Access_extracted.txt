1 AN ACT concerning
2 Common Ownership Communities – Recreational Common Areas – Sensitive
3 Information as Condition for Access
4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting a cooperative housing corporation, condominium
5 association, or homeowners association from requiring certain sensitive information
6 of certain individuals as a condition for access to a shared recreational area; and
7 generally relating to recreational common areas of cooperative housing corporations,
8 condominiums, and homeowners associations.
9 BY adding to
10 Article – Corporations and Associations
11 Section 5–6B–23.2
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2014 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)

6 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
7 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
8 Article – Corporations and Associations
9 5–6B–23.2.
10 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
11 INDICATED.
12 (2) “COMMON AREA” MEANS ANY AREA IN A COOPERATIVE PROJECT
13 IN WHICH MEMBERS HAVE A POSSESSORY INTEREST IN COMMON.
14 (3) (I) “SENSITIVE INFORMATION” MEANS AN INDIVIDUAL’S:
15 1. SOCIAL SECURITY CARD OR SOCIAL SECURITY
16 NUMBER;
17 2. INDIVIDUAL TAXPAYER IDENTIFICATION NUMBER;
18 3. BIRTH CERTIFICATE;
19 4. RACIAL OR ETHNIC ORIGIN;
20 5. NATIONAL ORIGIN;
21 6. CITIZENSHIP OR IMMIGRATION STATUS; OR
22 7. RELIGIOUS OR PHILOSOPHICAL BELIEFS; OR
23 8. MEDICAL RECORDS.
24 (II) “SENSITIVE INFORMATION” DOES NOT INCLUDE AN
25 INDIVIDUAL’S GOVERNMENT–ISSUED PHOTO IDENTIFICATION, SUCH AS A DRIVER’S
26 LICENSE.
27 (B) A COOPERATIVE HOUSING CORPORATION MAY NOT REQUIRE A MEMBER
28 OR UNIT OCCUPANT, OR THE GUEST OR CHILD OF A MEMBER OR UNIT OCCUPANT,

1 TO PROVIDE SENSITIVE INFORMATION AS A CONDITION FOR ACCESSING OR USING A
2 RECREATIONAL COMMON AREA, SUCH AS A READING LOUNGE, GAME ROOM,
3 PLAYGROUND, OR SWIMMING POOL.

5 11–108.2.
6 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION, “SENSITIVE INFORMATION” MEANS AN
7 INDIVIDUAL’S:
8 (I) SOCIAL SECURITY CARD OR SOCIAL SECURITY NUMBER;
9 (II) INDIVIDUAL TAXPAYER IDENTIFICATION NUMBER;
10 (III) BIRTH CERTIFICATE;
11 (IV) RACIAL OR ETHNIC ORIGIN;
12 (V) NATIONAL ORIGIN;
13 (VI) CITIZENSHIP OR IMMIGRATION STATUS; OR
14 (VII) RELIGIOUS OR PHILOSOPHICAL BELIEFS; OR
15 (VIII) MEDICAL RECORDS.
16 (2) “SENSITIVE INFORMATION” DOES NOT INCLUDE AN INDIVIDUAL’S
17 GOVERNMENT–ISSUED PHOTO IDENTIFICATION, SUCH AS A DRIVER’S LICENSE.
18 (B) A CONDOMINIUM MAY NOT REQUIRE A UNIT OWNER OR OCCUPANT, OR
19 THE GUEST OR CHILD OF A UNIT OWNER OR OCCUPANT, TO PROVIDE SENSITIVE
20 INFORMATION AS A CONDITION FOR ACCESSING OR USING A RECREATIONAL
21 COMMON AREA, SUCH AS A READING LOUNGE, GAME ROOM, PLAYGROUND, OR
22 SWIMMING POOL.

4 (VI) CITIZENSHIP OR IMMIGRATION STATUS; OR
5 (VII) RELIGIOUS OR PHILOSOPHICAL BELIEFS; OR
6 (VIII) MEDICAL RECORDS.
7 (2) “SENSITIVE INFORMATION” DOES NOT INCLUDE AN INDIVIDUAL’S
8 GOVERNMENT–ISSUED PHOTO IDENTIFICATION, SUCH AS A DRIVER’S LICENSE.
9 (B) A HOMEOWNERS ASSOCIATION MAY NOT REQUIRE A LOT OWNER OR
10 OCCUPANT, OR THE GUEST OR CHILD OF A LOT OWNER OR OCCUPANT, TO PROVIDE
11 SENSITIVE INFORMATION AS A CONDITION FOR ACCESSING OR USING A
12 RECREATIONAL COMMON AREA, SUCH AS A READING LOUNGE, GAME ROOM,
13 PLAYGROUND, OR SWIMMING POOL.
14 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
15 October 1, 2025.
