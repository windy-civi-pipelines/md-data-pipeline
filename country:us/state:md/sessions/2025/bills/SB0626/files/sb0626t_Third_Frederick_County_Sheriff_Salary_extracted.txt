CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Frederick County – Sheriff – Salary
3 FOR the purpose of altering the annual salary of the Sheriff of Frederick County to be equal
4 to the salary of a Department of State Police lieutenant colonel, at the highest
5 available step for a lieutenant colonel under the Department of State Police pay plan
6 a certain amount for a certain calendar year; altering the annual salary of the Sheriff
7 of Frederick County to be a certain percentage of the salary of the State’s Attorney
8 for Frederick County for certain subsequent calendar years; and generally relating
9 to the salary of the Sheriff of Frederick County.
10 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
11 Article – Courts and Judicial Proceedings
12 Section 2–324(a)
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
15 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
16 Article – Courts and Judicial Proceedings
17 Section 2–324(b)
18 Annotated Code of Maryland
19 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
20 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
21 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.

Underlining indicates amendments to bill.
Strike out indicates matter stricken from the bill by amendment or deleted from the law by

2 SENATE BILL 626
1 Article – Courts and Judicial Proceedings
2 2–324.
3 (a) This section applies only in Frederick County.
4 (b) (1) The SALARY FOR THE Sheriff of Frederick County [shall receive an
5 annual salary of [$125,000] IS EQUAL TO THE SALARY OF A DEPARTMENT OF STATE
6 POLICE LIEUTENANT COLONEL, AT THE HIGHEST AVAILABLE STEP FOR A
7 LIEUTENANT COLONEL UNDER THE DEPARTMENT OF STATE POLICE PAY PLAN IN
8 EFFECT ON THE DAY PRIOR TO THE DAY THAT THE SHERIFF BEGINS A TERM OF
9 OFFICE:
10 (I) $187,313 FOR CALENDAR YEAR 2025; AND
11 (II) 82.5% OF THE STATE’S ATTORNEY FOR FREDERICK
12 COUNTY’S ANNUAL SALARY INCLUDING ANY INCREMENTS AND SALARY
13 ADJUSTMENTS AUTHORIZED UNDER § 15–411(B)(2) OF THE CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
14 ARTICLE FOR CALENDAR YEAR 2026 AND EACH SUBSEQUENT CALENDAR YEAR.
15 (2) ANY CHANGE IN THE SALARY PAID UNDER THE DEPARTMENT OF
16 STATE POLICE PAY PLAN TO THE STATE’S ATTORNEY FOR FREDERICK COUNTY
17 DURING THE TERM OF OFFICE OF THE SHERIFF MAY NOT APPLY TO THE INCUMBENT
18 SHERIFF DURING THE SHERIFF’S CURRENT TERM OF OFFICE, BUT THE CHANGED
19 RATE SHALL TAKE EFFECT AT THE BEGINNING OF THE NEXT FOLLOWING TERM OF
20 OFFICE.
21 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That, in accordance with Article
22 III, § 35 of the Maryland Constitution, this Act may not be construed to extend or apply to
23 the salary or compensation of the Sheriff of Frederick County while serving in a term of
24 office beginning before the effective date of this Act, but the provisions of this Act
25 concerning the salary or compensation of the Sheriff of Frederick County shall take effect
26 at the beginning of the next following term of office. This limitation does not apply to an
27 individual appointed or elected after the effective date of this Act to fill out an unexpired
28 term.
29 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
30 October 1, 2025.
