10 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
11 Article – Economic Development
12 2.5–110.
13 (A) THERE IS A NONPROFIT ORGANIZATIONS NAVIGATOR IN THE
14 DEPARTMENT.
15 (B) THE SECRETARY SHALL DESIGNATE THE NAVIGATOR.
16 (C) THE NAVIGATOR SHALL:
17 (1) PROVIDE TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE TO NONPROFIT
18 ORGANIZATIONS IN OBTAINING ACCESS TO AND APPLYING FOR STATE GRANT
19 PROGRAMS;
20 (2) WORK WITH NONPROFIT ORGANIZATIONS AND UNITS OF STATE
21 GOVERNMENT TO RESOLVE PROCEDURAL COMPLEXITIES AND DELAYS IN STATE
22 GRANT–MAKING PROCESSES;
23 (3) COLLECT AND SHARE INFORMATION ON OPPORTUNITIES FOR
24 FEDERAL, STATE, AND LOCAL GRANTS WITH NONPROFIT ORGANIZATIONS; AND
25 (4) REPRESENT NONPROFIT ORGANIZATIONS’ INTERESTS AND
26 CONCERNS AS A MEMBER OF THE MARYLAND EFFICIENT GRANT APPLICATION
27 COUNCIL ESTABLISHED UNDER § 2–209 OF THE STATE FINANCE AND
28 PROCUREMENT ARTICLE.
29 (D) THE NAVIGATOR MAY NOT PROVIDE SERVICES TO A NONPROFIT
30 ORGANIZATION IN THE STATE THAT PROVIDES A TOTAL ANNUAL COMPENSATION
31 EXCEEDING $250,000 TO ANY SINGLE SALARIED EMPLOYEE.
