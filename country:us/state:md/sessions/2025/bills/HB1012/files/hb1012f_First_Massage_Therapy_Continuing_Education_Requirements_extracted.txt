Section 1:
J2 5lr2946
By: Delegate Kerr
Introduced and read first time: February 3, 2025
Assigned to: Health and Government Operations
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Massage Therapy – Continuing Education Requirements
3 FOR the purpose of requiring the State Board of Massage Therapy Examiners to establish
4 certain continuing education requirements; authorizing the Board to refuse to renew
5 the license of a licensed massage therapist who fails to attend the required course or
6 submit certification of attendance at the required courses; and generally relating to
7 continuing education requirements for licensed massage therapists.
8 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
9 Article – Health Occupations
10 Section 6–101(a), (b), (d), (e), and (i)
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
13 BY adding to
14 Article – Health Occupations
15 Section 6–305.1
16 Annotated Code of Maryland
17 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2024 Supplement)
18 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
19 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
20 Article – Health Occupations
21 6–101.
22 (a) In this title the following words have the meanings indicated.
23 (b) “Board” means the State Board of Massage Therapy Examiners.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb1012*
2 HOUSE BILL 1012
1 (d) “License” means, unless the context requires otherwise, a license issued by the
2 Board to practice massage therapy.
3 (e) “Licensed massage therapist” means an individual who is licensed by the
4 Board to practice massage therapy.
5 (i) “Supervised hands–on techniques” means massage techniques that are
6 performed under the direct, in–person supervision of a qualified licensed instructor who is
7 on–site in:
8 (1) A classroom;
9 (2) A laboratory;
10 (3) A school clinic; or
11 (4) A health care setting.
12 6–305.1.
13 (A) IN ADDITION TO ANY OTHER QUALIFICATIONS AND REQUIREMENTS
14 ESTABLISHED BY THE BOARD, THE BOARD SHALL ESTABLISH CONTINUING
15 EDUCATION REQUIREMENTS AS A CONDITION OF THE RENEWAL OF A LICENSE
16 UNDER THIS TITLE.
17 (B) THE BOARD SHALL ADOPT REGULATIONS TO:
18 (1) APPROVE CONTINUING EDUCATION COURSES REQUIRED UNDER
19 THIS SECTION; AND
20 (2) ESTABLISH THE NUMBER OF HOURS OF CONTINUING EDUCATION
21 REQUIRED, AT LEAST HALF OF WHICH MUST INCLUDE SUPERVISED HANDS–ON
22 TECHNIQUES.
23 (C) AT THE TIME A LICENSEE APPLIES FOR LICENSE RENEWAL, THE
24 APPLICANT SHALL SUBMIT TO THE BOARD, ON A FORM PROVIDED BY THE BOARD, A
25 CERTIFICATION THAT THE APPLICANT HAS ATTENDED THE REQUIRED COURSES.
26 (D) THE BOARD MAY REFUSE TO RENEW THE LICENSE OF A LICENSED
27 MASSAGE THERAPIST WHO HAS:
28 (1) FAILED TO ATTEND THE REQUIRED COURSES; OR
29 (2) FAILED TO SUBMIT CERTIFICATION OF ATTENDANCE AT THE
30 REQUIRED COURSES.
HOUSE BILL 1012 3
1 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
2 1, 2026.
